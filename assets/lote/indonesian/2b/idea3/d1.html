Dewi has her interview for her scholarship coming up, and I have my interview at Tasik Restaurant, so it's a good time for me to review interview techniques. Dewi hasn't had a formal interview before, and her interview is going to be with Australians and she doesn't know what language it will be in, so she's pretty stressed. I have attached the tips we put together.

Oh ya, Dewi, do you know that in Western culture handshakes are quite different? In Indonesia, a really limp handshake shows that you are 'halus', doesn't it? A strong handshake means you're not very refined. But it's the opposite here and Westerners find the Indonesian hand-stroke kinda off-putting. So if you have Australian interviewers, show that you know something about Australian culture by having a firm handshake! (And I'll practise the limp handshake for my interview!) Jangan lupa, ya?
