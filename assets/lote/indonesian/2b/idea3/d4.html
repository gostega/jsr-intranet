Hi Dewi. In Australia, there is strict Equal Employment Opportunity legislation (EEO legislation) which prevents discrimination. For example, those job advertisements you posted that have ‘wanita, max 25th’ would not be allowed in Australia. There are strict guidelines about questions that employers are or aren't allowed to ask in interviews. For example, they aren't allowed to ask about your marital status, sexuality, political affiliations, personal habits or anything like that. They aren't even allowed to ask how old you are (but most people put it on their CV anyway) or request a photo.  For more information, you can search the web for 'Equal Employment Opportunity legislation'. It differs a bit from state to state. I hope this helps. What about in Indonesia?
