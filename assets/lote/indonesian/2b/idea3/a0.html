Oral communication (Listening and Responding, Spoken Interaction)

You will listen to Dewi’s interview for student exchange (which she recorded secretly) and fill in a response sheet. You should read the response sheet carefully first so that you know what to listen for. You will only be given a certain amount of time to listen to the interview – it may be 20 minutes, it may be longer. Tanyalah kepada guru Anda.

After listening to Dewi's interview, you will discuss it with another panel member of the scholarship board. There will then be further questions on a range of topics.
