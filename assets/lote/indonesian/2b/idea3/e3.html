Wah, latihan bagus Ibu Indah, dan selamat pada Brendon, Brooke dan Kerry atas wawancaranya yang baik. Nah, inilah usul saya:

Could you use the warm/cool feedback activity to evaluate your own performance in an interview or oral assessment? (Or you could ask a friend you feel comfortable with to evaluate you.)

It is useful to record yourself doing an interview, and, when watching it, see if you can pick out some points to congratulate yourself on, as well as some points for improvement. It may feel uncomfortable, but you will be amazed at what you notice that you didn’t realise you were doing at the time!
