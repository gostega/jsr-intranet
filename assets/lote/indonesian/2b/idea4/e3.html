There is a lot of hard language in this article! Remember: you don’t need to understand it all to make meaning from the text. Here are some exercises to help you.

Tentang studi

From the explanations in the section ‘Tentang Studi’, create a table of the acronyms, their expanded terms and their meaning (in English) related to campus life. The English and the first example have been done for you:

KRS

Kartu Rencana Studi

Study plan describing which units you are taking and the number of credit points

SKS

Satuan Kredit Semester

Semester study load or limit, which usually starts at 18 units then is adjusted depending on how many units the student passed in the previous semester.

IP

Indeks Prestasi

 The accumulated score.

IPS

Indeks Prestasi Semester

The accumulated score for a semester.

IPK

Indeks Prestasi Kumulatif

The accumulated score for the year.

PA

Pembimbing Akademis

A role similar to a combination of form teacher and guidance counsellor in a school.

SP

Semester Pendek

An additional, shorter study time, usually between semesters, when students can catch up on subjects they missed, failed or want to improve.

KHS

Kartu Hasil Studi

Similar to a school report, it lists the grades (A-E) earned for each subject studied in the semester.
