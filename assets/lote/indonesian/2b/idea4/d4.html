Dari Dewi:

Hehehe, memang membuat kesalahan itu wajar kalau belajar bahasa asing.

Tontonan artinya ‘a show’ atau ‘performance’.

Menunjukkan adalah kata kerja ‘to show’

So you said ‘I will (be) a performance you the table’. LOL. Yang seharusnya dikatakan adalah ‘saya akan menunjukkan meja’.

When a word in English can be either a noun or a verb (eg to show / a show; to visit / a visit) you have to make sure you use the matching term in Indonesian.

First, decide if the English word you are using is a noun or a verb. Then look for the Indonesian noun or verb in the dictionary. Often, verbs start with me- / ber- and nouns end in -an.

In the dictionary, the noun is usually given first.
So if you look up ‘show’, you will find:

  • tontonan/pertunjukan – a show/performance

  • menunjukkan – to show.

Hati-hati! Not all nouns end in -an and not all verbs start with me- or ber-. Base-words can be verbs or nouns.
