How does Kerry ask Dewi to slow down?

'Dewi bisa berbicara pelan-pelan?' is what Kerry actually says. It would have been better to say: 'Tolong bicara pelan-pelan' because 'tolong' is better to use than 'bisa' in this situation. Obviously, Dewi bisa bicara pelan pelan, but Kerry is asking for help, so tolong is more correct.

Here are two further exercises.

Firstly, identify two things Dewi finds unusual or surprising in each room. Prepare to discuss these things.

Secondly, rewatch the section in the kitchen, making detailed notes about what goes on. Focus on the sequence of events, the explanations made by each speaker, and how you think each person felt at different times. You will need these notes later on.
