Remember your viewing strategies! Don’t expect to understand everything the first time. Watch and listen to get a feel for the pace and topic of conversation the first time. Know what sort of information you are looking for by reading any questions or prompts first. Then watch again, listening for key words.

Here are some questions to test your understanding.

Tontonlah permulaan wawancara dengan Dini and Heru, dan jawablah pertanyaan berikut dalam bahasa Indonesia.


1. Mengapa Dini dan Heru datang ke Perth?

Mereka ambil kursus pendek di Universitas Curtin.


2. Heru dan Dini bekerja di bidang apa?

Mereka bekerja di bidang pendidikan (untuk pemerintahan, kerjanya instructional design dan penataran guru).


3. Dini dan Heru berasal dari mana?

Dini berasal dari Bandung, Heru berasal dari Palangka Raya, Kalimantan Tengah.


4. What do you notice about the handshake, especially from the two women? (Answer in English.)

Indonesian handshakes are typically soft rather than strong. Many people touch their hand to their heart after meeting and shaking hands with someone to indicate that they have accepted them.


Now watch the second interview and answer the following questions.

5. Pertanyaan apa yang ditanyakan oleh pewawancara?

Oleh-oleh apa yang ingin Anda bawa pulang?


6. Penjelasan apa yang diminta oleh Dewi?

Bentuk atau informasi? Goods/physical objects or information?
