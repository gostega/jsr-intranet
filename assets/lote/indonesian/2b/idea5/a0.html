Tugas 2

Written communication

Your task is to design a brochure or article in Indonesian providing travel advice for Indonesians visiting your area, focusing on one of the following aspects of tourism:

  • hotel
  • tempat belanja
  • obyek wisata
  • restoran.

You need to provide information about, and advise tourists on, three hotels/shopping areas/tourist attractions/restaurants.

You should:

  • compare venues
  • provide advice
  • identify any points of information particularly of relevance to Indonesian visitors.

Make sure you use description, full sentences, good structure, and some complex grammar.
