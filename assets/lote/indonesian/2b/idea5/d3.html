Please!!!

Dari Dewi:

Australians seem to use ‘please’ a lot. There is no one word for ‘please’ in Indonesian. Instead there is a variety of words used for different contexts and purposes.

Kata Keterangan Contoh
tolong Please help me by doing this.  Tolong buka pintu.
harap Please / you are expected to (do this). Most often used in formal situation, from someone in authority to someone with less authority. Tamu harap lapor.
silakan Please (do something that is going to benefit you). Silakan duduk.
dipersilakan More like ‘you are kindly invited to’. Anda dipersilakan duduk.
minta More like ‘request’. Minta kecap.
boleh saya … May I …? (very polite) Boleh saya titip pesan?

Dewi benar. The words ‘please’ and ‘thank you’ are not used as commonly in Indonesian as they are in English. In Indonesia, you do not normally thank someone when what they have done falls clearly within what they are supposed to do (eg their job).
