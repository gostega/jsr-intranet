Okay, I’m getting the hang of this abbreviation thing in job ads. Most often the vowels are dropped, eg:

  • wnt = wanita
  • pr = pria
  • prsh = perusahaan
  • lmr = lamar
  • lkp = lengkap
  • bbrp = beberapa
  • tk = tukang

If you know the main words, it’s pretty easy to guess. (Kata-kata umum ada di pojok bahasa.)

If the word starts with ‘m’ or a ‘p’ and you can’t guess the second letter, there’s a good chance it’s an ‘e’ (for me- words or pe- words), eg:

  • mrwat = merawat
  • prwat = perawat
