It’s a training school, like a college or TAFE, for flight attendants and other airline staff. The phrase Mutu terjamin, biaya terjangkau, tempat terbatas, confused me a bit until Dewi reminded me to take the te- prefix off. In these cases, ter- means ‘can be’ or ‘is in a state of being’.

Here are some examples of the use of the ter- prefix:

batas

limit

terbatas

is limited

jangkau

reach

terjangkau

can be reached/attainable

jamin

guarantee

terjamin

is guaranteed/can be guarenteed

hormat

respect

terhormat

is respected

sedia

prepare

tersedia

is prepared

sebut

mention

tersebut

stated/mentioned

ancam

threat(en)

terancam

threatened (eg with extinction)

tulis

write

tertulis

written

lampir

attach

terlampir

attached

Formal version: Paket Kerja di Jepang. Pabrik resmi cari Tenaga Kerja Indonesia. Job di pabrik. Visa Magang Kerja. Biaya ringan. Pasti berangkat. Garansi 100%. Hubungi: 6771535.

Indonesia is one of the most populous nations in the world. Labour is cheap, and many multinational companies have factories (pabrik) in Indonesia because cheap labour keeps production costs down. Some of these labourers get paid as little as $2.50 for an eight-hour work day.

One option for those at the lower end of the employment ladder is finding work overseas as an unskilled labourer. TKI, or Tenaga Kerja Indonesia, is the term used to refer to the Indonesian workforce overseas. These people take low-paid, low-prestige jobs such as factory workers in Japan, or as maids/servants in Singapore or Malaysia.

Often the work conditions for such jobs are lower than workers from those countries themselves would tolerate; therefore, cheap labour is imported from countries such as Indonesia and the Philippines. Working conditions may be better than in Indonesia, and it is seen as an opportunity to escape from a life of poverty, and have a chance at a life in a ‘rich’ country. However, taking such work is not without risks, as unscrupulous companies take advantage of poverty and desperation. The ‘low fee’ may be astronomical by the standards of these workers, and workers will sign a contract to the effect that they pay the company back over a number of months, effectively imprisoning them in their job. Women may be unlucky enough to apply for a factory job and then find themselves sold into the sex industry. There are regular news articles about TKI suffering abuse such as beatings, and there is little chance for escape or legal recourse for those at the bottom of the social ladder. Therefore advertisements will state: Garansi 100% Pabrik resmi. Whether they are or not, the worker is unlikely to find out until they get there. Still, many are willing to take the risk to escape from the poverty of their home situation.
