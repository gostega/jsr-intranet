List the three jobs you would least like to apply for.

In Indonesian, in a Microsoft® Word document, explain why you would like to apply for those jobs, or why you would be suited to them. Some useful phrases include:

saya ingin melamar untuk ...

I wish to apply for ...

pekerjaan sebagai ...

work as a ...

jabatan ini ...

this position ...

karena ...

because ...

saya seorang yang ...

I am a person who ...

Also check the pojok bahasa for more useful phrases.
