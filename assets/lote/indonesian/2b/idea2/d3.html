Re: Kriteria lamaran, dari Ibu_Indah pada jam 12:10.

Hati-hati!  When you are using your dictionary, make sure you choose the most appropriate word. There may be several meanings for one word, and therefore several options. For example, the word ‘address’ could refer to:

  1. the physical address / location of a business
  2. a direction to address the selection criteria.

Therefore you need to look through the whole dictionary entry for the best word to use.

If there is not a close example in the dictionary, try to think of a more accurate word in English, or a different way of saying it. For example, you may change ‘he addressed the audience’ to ‘he gave a speech to the audience’.

The word could also be a noun (my address is) or a verb (address the letter to ...), in which case a different affixation could be necessary (eg me-/ber- for verbs). Again, look at the whole entry to find the word closest to the one you wish to use.

Choose from the dropdown menus the correct word for each part in the sentence. Use your dictionary to help you.
