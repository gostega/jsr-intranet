Me- verbs

Verbs (action/doing words) in Indonesian often have either a ‘ber-’ or ‘me-’ prefix. There are so many words that can have the ‘me-’ prefix on them that there could be a whole dictionary full of ‘me-’ words. So to avoid the ‘M’ section of the dictionary being bigger than the rest of the dictionary, words are listed in the dictionary under their base-word.

So if you see a word you don’t know starting with me, you need to know where to find it in the dictionary.

There are five me- prefixes assigned to the different letters of the alphabet. (Two letters of the alphabet are missing from the table below. If you look in your dictionary, you will see why!)
