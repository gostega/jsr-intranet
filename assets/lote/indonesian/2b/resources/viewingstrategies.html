Viewing strategies

  • Relax and recognise that you are going to need to view the text a few times to make sense of it. A relaxed, positive mind is more receptive than a stressed-out, ‘I can’t do this’ mind!
  • View a text the first time for visual and contextual clues that help you guess the meaning of what is going on.
  • View the text a second time, listening carefully for aural clues. Jot down key words and phrases.
  • When you make notes, listen for key words and write them down in Indonesian. Don’t try to translate them immediately – it will take too long and you will miss the next few key words or clues. Go over them after you have finished viewing to make sense of them.
  • Replay, looking and listening for more information.
  • Enjoy watching!
