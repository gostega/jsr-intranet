When you start writing your own CV as Brendon requested, you can find templates online or in Microsoft® Word, and Indonesianise them. Just don’t forget the cultural elements of CVs that Brendon mentioned in his earlier post!

It’s a good idea to turn the language on Microsoft® Word to Indonesian by clicking Tools, Language, Set language (or follow the instructions from the Help menu). Hati-hati! This DOES NOT spellcheck Indonesian, but it should stop auto-correcting words incorrectly. Be sure to have a friend proofread your work carefully!
