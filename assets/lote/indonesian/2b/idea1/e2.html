Re: Ringkasan Brendon, dari IndoGuRu pada jam 10:56.

Hati-hati! Tarik means pull.

Menarik means ‘interesting’. It comes from the phrase menarik hati, which literally means ‘pulls at the heart’.

To say ‘interested in’, you need to say tertarik akan or tertarik pada.

Be careful not to confuse the me- and the ter- form of the word!
