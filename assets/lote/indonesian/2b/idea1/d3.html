Re: Cita-cita dan aspirasi, dari Ibu_Indah pada jam 22:21.

There were a few ke-an nouns in that conversation! Ke-an words are always nouns. You’ll probably run into a few more when looking through things to do with jobs. Mudah-mudahan penjelasan ini bisa membantu Anda.

  • When you add the affix ke-an to an adjective like happy/sad/clever, it turns it into the abstract noun form of that word (happiness/sadness/cleverness).
  • The exception to the rule is malu which does not become ‘embarrassment’. Check your dictionary to find out what it means! If you use it incorrectly, you certainly will feel malu!
