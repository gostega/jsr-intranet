Equal Opportunities Employment laws require that you cannot discriminate on the basis of appearance (cantik, putih), gender (wanita), age (max 30th) skin colour (putih) or religion (muslim). You can also only specify height in certain cases, where there is an Occupational Health and Safety issue. It is very unlikely you would be asked to send a photo with a job application.
