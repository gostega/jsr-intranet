Welcome to Indonesian Unit 2B – Bisa saya bantu?

The focus of this unit is Bisa saya bantu? (Can I help you?). Brendon is an Australian student who has decided to take a year off after school to decide what he wants to do next. He knows that he wants to do something with his Indonesian language study but he isn’t sure what. In the meantime, he wants to keep up his Indonesian skills and find a job.

Brendon’s chat-friend, Dewi, lives in Jakarta and is in her first year of journalism at university. She wants to improve her English in order to increase her chances at getting a job in the media industry in Jakarta.

Brendon and Dewi decide to develop a web log together to help each other out with their aims for the year and to help out anyone else who is interested in work opportunities, student exchange or Indonesian studies. Two interested Indonesian language teachers mentor Brendon and Dewi along the way.

You will work through Brendon and Dewi’s blog to cover the following topics:

  • Karir apa? (careers)
  • Lamaran (applications)
  • Wawancara (interviews)
  • Pertukaran siswa (student exchange)
  • Turisme (tourism)
  • Peduli Indonesia (aid and relief work in Indonesia).

Each topic contains four sections:

Introduce – includes a video from either Brendon or Dewi giving an overview of the topic and tasks. From the Introduce page, you can launch their web log, which contains the Discover, Engage, and Apply sections.

Discover – contains the essential content you will need to communicate in Indonesian.

Engage – provides you with a range of activities to help you practise using the new content.

Apply – gives you an opportunity to apply your skills and knowledge.

Once you launch a blog, it will open up in a new window. Click on the links under each section on the right hand side to learn more. Close the current blog window to return to the main menu when you want to select another topic.

Let’s begin with the topic – Karir apa?
