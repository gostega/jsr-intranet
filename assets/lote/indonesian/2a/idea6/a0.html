Apply what you have learnt from the Discover and Engage sections to complete the following LRS assessment task.

Tugas – Dengarkanlah percakapan!

Listen and respond to a telephone conversation. This will be provided by your teacher.

Tugas – Pesan

  1. Listen to a voicemail message and note the main points communicated.
  2. In Indonesian, write a SMS responding to the message, saying when you will call. Remember to use the appropriate register (level of language) for the person to whom you are sending the SMS.
  3. Decide what you are going to say when you make the telephone call.
  4. Predict the types of responses you may encounter, and think about how you will respond.

Tugas – Role play

Make a phone call (real or role-played) to discuss and justify your plans.
Tips:

  • Jot down some points you may wish to refer to (eg asking if they received your SMS).
  • Remember to consider the politeness conventions you have learnt, such as the appropriate words to use when addressing someone older than you.
