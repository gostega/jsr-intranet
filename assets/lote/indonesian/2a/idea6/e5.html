Tugas 5

Now you have practised a telephone conversation with guidance, it is time to plan your own telephone conversation. Complete the table below to determine the following key points of information:

Purpose of phone call (eg. book a hotel room, asking about homework)

Context (formal? Informal?)

Age of person/people you are likely to speak with

Relationship (Close? Have met but don’t know well? Haven’t met?)

Terms of address to use

Level of language to use (formal/informal)

Key information to communicate/obtain

Conventions to use

Note down some key phrases you will use to communicate.


With someone else, role-play the conversation. If you are practising it with another student, tell them the purpose of the phone call, the role they will be playing and give them a few minutes to prepare.
