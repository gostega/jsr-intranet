Bikin flik-flak

Write eight short or four longer sentences to make a prediction. Use the words from the Pojok bahasa for Ramalan.

Link words that may be useful for creating longer sentences or joining sentences include:

  • akibatnya – consequently (eg this week, your boyfriend/girlfriend will be annoying. Consequently, you will break up with them)
  • walaupun – although (eg although you break up with your pacar this week, you will get together again on Friday)
  • meskipun begitu/walaupun begitu – although that is so
  • oleh karena itu – because of that.

Other things to remember are:

  • Each sentence should include a time frame to indicate when the prediction is going to take place (for example minggu ini/tahun ini/bulan depan).
  • Don’t forget to use an adverb like ‘akan’ or ‘seharusnya’ to indicate that something will or should happen! For example:

                        Minggu depan kamu akan jatuh cinta.

Follow the instructions in Bikin flik-flak. Write your predictions into the large spaces in the flik-flak.
