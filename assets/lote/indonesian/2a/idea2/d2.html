Now you know that ‘sesuatu yang’ means ‘something that is’ and ‘seorang yang’ means ‘someone who is’, you can use this to help yourself when you are at a loss for a word in speaking Indonesian. This is called circumlocution. For example, you’re in a shop wanting to find out where you can buy insect repellent, but you don’t know how to say it.

You might say:
‘Permisi, saya cari ... anu ... saya lupa bahasa Indonesianya, tapi saya cari sesuatu yang dipakai supaya tidak digigit nyamuk’.
