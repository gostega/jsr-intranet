To ‘di-‘ or not to ‘di-‘?

You already know that in passive sentences, the me-/ber- prefix is not used. If the sentence is:

  • ‘di-’ use di-
    For example: Kelas 11 diajar oleh guru.
  • drop the ‘me-’ (use the base form of the verb).
    For example: Kelas 11 saya ajar.
