Wawancara artinya interview.

In this topic you will read a magazine interview with an Indonesian celebrity. Some of the questions asked may be different to what you would expect to see in a celebrity interview in an Australian magazine. This will give you an insight into Indonesian life, beliefs and attitudes.

Outcomes

  • You will be able to ask and respond to questions, identifying question words, keywords and ‘fillers’.
  • You will be able to use basic object focus in questions.
  • You will read and write a magazine article about an interview.
  • You will learn to use ‘fillers’ in conversation.
  • You will use a model text as a basis for creating your own text.

You will engage with the following text types:

  • magazine interviews
  • conversation
  • short audio texts.
