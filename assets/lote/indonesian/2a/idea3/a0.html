Tugas – Kompetisi Seleb Sehari-hari

Kompetisi! Ada rubrik baru, lho! Majalah Sini! akan menyajikan rubrik baru setiap bulan, namanya Seleb Sehari-hari. Ya, sebenarnya itu wawancara dengan pembaca ... Pembaca pertama yang di-feature akan meraih hadiah 500,000 rupiah. Asyik, ‘kan? Kalau mau ikut kompetisi ini, tulislah sebuah wawancara (seperti wawancara dengan Rizky Suprapto, lho) untuk di-feature dalam edisi Sini! bulan depan.

Bersediakah kamu?

Summary: Sini! magazine is creating a new rubrik called Selebriti Sehari-hari based on their existing article. You are to be the first selebriti featured. You need to create an article (including a wawancara) for yourself.

Step 1: Imagine you are being asked the same questions you saw in the Indonesian Idol magazine article. Answer them yourself in Indonesian.
Step 2: Create two additional questions using the object focus format and write answers to them.
Step 3: Write an introduction to the article as though you were the journalist interviewing yourself. Use some of the conventions you have studied. For example:
             Cewek yang akrab dipanggil Kazza ini …
Step 4: Proofread, edit and ask questions about any points you are unsure of.
Step 5: Serahkan!

Tip: Notice in the written article how many times the word ‘you’ is used. Copy this
  convention in to your own interview to make your writing more authentic.
