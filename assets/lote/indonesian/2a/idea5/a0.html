Text alternative

Complete the following 4 exercises and submit for assessment.

  1. Estimate how old you think the writer is and give reasons for why you thought that.
  2. Describe the sort of student you think the writer is and give reasons for your answers.
  3. What preparations do you think the writer made for his journey(s)?
  4. In Indonesian, either:
    1. Complete the paragraph that starts ‘Mudik ketiga. Saya sudah menjadi mahasiswa ITB. Saat mudik adalah saat-saat yang....’ with your own creative account.
    2. Choose 6–10 signs from the site www.foto-foto.com/asigns/index.html and write a story about your experience travelling to a village in Indonesia, using the signs or images you have chosen as stimuli.
    3. Write a passage for a website/article explaining why you would or wouldn’t ikut mudik.

Tulislah kurang-lebih 150 kata dalam bahasa Indonesia.

Tugas – Percakapan

Read the following questions and practise answering them aloud. Remember, your brain, tongue and ear need practice working together to speak Indonesian. Reading and writing is not enough!

  • Kalau Anda berlibur, Anda suka berlibur ke mana? 
  • Di mana tempat yang menarik untuk berlibur?
  • Seandainya Anda tidak tinggal bersama keluarga Anda, Anda ingin tinggal di mana?
  • Apa yang harus disiapkan bila Anda ingin mengadakan perjalanan panjang, perjalanan ke luar negeri misalnya/contohnya.
  • Pernahkah Anda ke Indonesia?
  • Apakah yang dimaksud dengan istilah ‘mudik’ di Indonesia?
  • Kapan kebanyakan orang di Indonesia pulang kampung?
