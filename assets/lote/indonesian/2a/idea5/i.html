Pulang mudik is a big event in Indonesia, although it is not always called that outside of Java. You will find out what mudik is in the Discover section, and read a variety of texts related to mudik in the Engage section.

Outcomes

  • You will be able to give instructions and make suggestions.
  • You will recognise commands and instructions, and be able to choose the right word or affix to give them yourself.
  • You will read longer and more complex texts.

You will engage with the following text types:

  • websites, weblogs and articles about mudik
  • photos
  • signs.
