This blog clearly demonstrates the high regard in which Indonesians hold family. The concept of Kekeluargaan, or 'familyness' (family values), is revered in Indonesia, and has even been understood to have a place in politics. Soeharto came under great criticism from the West, and eventually from within Indonesia, for the favouritism shown to his children in allocating business deals. The defence to this was that the West does not understand the 'konsep kekeluargaan yang ada di Indonesia'.
