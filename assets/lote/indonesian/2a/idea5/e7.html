Vita mentions that Universitas Trisakti Jakarta is kampus pahlawan reformasi which means ‘Campus of the Heroes of the Revolution’. On 12 May 1998, during a massive student demonstration against the Soeharto government, four students were shot dead in the demonstrations. This led to further demonstrations, and the resignation of Soeharto on 21 May 1998.
