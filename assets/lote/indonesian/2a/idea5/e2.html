Tugas 2

Of the photos of signs you have seen so far, pick the three that you feel are most important. Copy and paste them into a Word document. Explain in Indonesian:

  • why you have chosen those signs
  • why you feel they may be important
  • what the consequences may be for not following them.

Check the Pojok bahasa for words to do with future and consequences.

Your writing may be creative or factual.
