Penilaian - based on the text above

1. Explain how old you think the writer is and why you think that.
Suggested answer: He may be a teenager or young adult or maybe older. He's travelling alone or with a friend. He describes the people near him as old or little (adik kecil). He has an MP3 player and Disney key ring, which means he's probably not very old, but he's reading a newspaper (koran). He's flown to other places like Paris and Frankfurt, so has some experience.

2. Does the writer like children? Explain the reasons for your answer.
Yes. He amuses the child in front and seems worried about her choking. He is sorry he hasn't got his Disney key ring to amuse her. He mentions the children several times.

3. Imagine you are the writer of this weblog. Write an introduction about yourself in Indonesian to post on the homepage of your blog. Describe yourself as fully as possible, using information you have inferred from the text about personality, age, likes and dislikes, etc. Write 100-150 words in Indonesian.
