Mohon maaf lahir dan batin

Mudik is an integral part of the celebrations at the end of Ramadan. The celebrations are known as Lebaran (or Idul Fitri). One of the most important traditions of Lebaran is asking the forgiveness of your family, friends and acquaintances for any wrongs you may have committed, by word or deed, unintentionally, throughout the year.

At Lebaran time, Muslims usually give one another greeting cards, e-cards, emails or SMS with the words: Mohon maaf lahir dan batin. As the cost of sending SMS in Indonesia is much cheaper than sending a card, an SMS has become the most common form of mohon maaf.

The practices of fasting and asking forgiveness are supposed to cleanse both the body and the mind, and give you a clean start for the new Islamic year.

Mohon is a very polite request.
