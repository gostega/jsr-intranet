Apply what you have learnt from the Discover and Engage sections to complete the following tasks.

Tugas – Sampul

View the covers and contents pages from two editions of Hai magazine, one of the leading teen magazines in Indonesia. Complete the brainstorm sheet – Sampul majalah individually, with a partner, or in a group.

Reproduced with the permission of Hai magazine.

This sheet is a starting point for mapping your progress and understanding. If you do this at the beginning of the semester, and again at the end, you will be able to see clearly the new information and skills you have learnt, and the understandings you have confirmed or consolidated.

Tugas – Penilaian

Write an email in Indonesian to a friend using slang. It can be about anything you like. Copy the email to your teacher.

OR

Create an A4 poster or a Microsoft PowerPoint® presentation in Indonesian giving advice about when to use (and when not to use) bahasa gaul. (Remember to use words such as harus, jangan and the -lah affix to give orders.) For example:
        Jangan menggunakan bahasa gaul dengan guru Anda.

Tugas – Diskusi

Prepare for an online voice-chat session with some of your Indonesian
e-pals. Prepare four questions for your friends, for example ‘what did you do on the weekend?’, and four comments about your own week.
