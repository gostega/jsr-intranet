Kapan pakai bahasa gaul?

It is really important that you are aware that language changes according to the context of the situation. (For example, you probably speak differently to your friends than you do to your grandmother.)

In Indonesian, slang is used between young people or people who have a close relationship with one another. Slang indicates closeness of relationship. It does not indicate the high level of respect Indonesians accord to older people, parents, or authority figures. You should not use slang with your teacher, someone you don’t know very well, people older than you, or someone in a formal workplace situation.
