Welcome to Indonesian Unit 2A – Saat ini, aku di sini

This unit is based around the idea of a majalah interaktif or an interactive, online magazine. This edition of the magazine has a tema khusus, which is ikut mudik. You’ll find out what ikut mudik is when you explore that topic. There is also a special feature on ramalan, and several other sections of the magazine.

You will not just be reading the magazine – there are lots of interactive activities, including entering some kompetisi to publish in the magazine yourself. You’ll also be able to have some discussions about the ‘here and now’ of life – your life, and the lives of teenagers in Indonesia.

There are seven topics listed on the left hand side. Each topic contains four sections:

Introduce (I) – provides an overview of the topic.
Discover (D) – contains the content.
Engage (E) – provides you with a range of activities related to the content.
Apply (A) – gives you an opportunity to apply your skills and knowledge. 

Click on the I, D, E & A on the top right hand corner to navigate between these four sections.
