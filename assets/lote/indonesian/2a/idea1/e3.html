Bahasa SMS…

When you write an SMS, you shorten words as much as possible. Indonesian slang also does this phonetically (according to how the words sound). For example:

gue (I)gw
mau mo
email imel
nggak gak ga
akan kan (hati-hati lho! Kan can also be bukan)
