Rubrik nasehat

Dr Diego, penulis rubrik nasehat yang terkenal di Indonesia bekerja untuk beberapa majalah. Dia begitu sibuk sehingga dia harus mempekerjakan asisten. Anda harus membaca tiga surat, sorotilah poin-poin pokoknya dan balaslah satu atau dua surat.

In this activity, you will read three different letters requesting Dr Diego’s advice, highlight the main issues. Respond to one or two of them yourself.

Launch interaction     Text alternative

Tulislah!

Either:
  1. Choose two of the three advice letters and write a reply of approximately 100 words in Indonesian.
    Or

  2. Choose one of the three advice letters and write a reply of approximately 200 words in Indonesian.
