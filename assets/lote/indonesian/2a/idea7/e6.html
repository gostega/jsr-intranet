Jokjay

Here are two jokes from Hai magazine. These jokes are set out as short narratives. Notice the number of time markers used to sequence the stories, such as sedang, sambil, akhirnya, sudah and tiba-tiba.

Your task is to explain the gist of one or both of the jokes in English. The second joke has a few more difficult words, if you are ready for a challenge!

Makan kacang

Bacalah joke berjudul ‘makan kacang’ dari majalah Hai. Inilah beberapa kata berguna supaya Anda dapat mengerti joke itu.

menjenguk look in on
dirawat nursed
ternyata evidently
mangkuk plate
kacang mede cashews
keasikan busily
cucu grandchild


Joke summary in English:

A guy drops in to check on his grandma in hospital, and finds her asleep. There is a bowl of cashews by the bed, so he eats them to pass the time. When grandma wakes up, he realises he has eaten them all and apologises. Grandma says ‘It's okay, I can't eat nuts any more, so I just suck the chocolate off all the cashews then put them in the bowl by my bed'.

Joke summary in English:

A rusty old atheist explorer is surrounded by a group of cannibals. He calls out to God, saying ‘God, I'm in danger'. God says ‘You're not in danger. Throw a rock at the one in front of you. He is the leader. If you can destroy him, the others will retreat in fear'. The explorer throws a rock; the leader is hit and falls down. The others draw their weapons. In a panic, he hears the heavenly voice again ‘NOW you're in danger.'
