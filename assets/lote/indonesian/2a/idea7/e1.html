Iklan HP

1. What selling techniques are employed in this advertisement?

  • Direct address and questioning of reader (Suka cokelat? Pastiii deh!).
  • Use of imperative – belilah!
  • Incentives (free chocolate, credit, wallpaper, games, and bag for phone).
2. What conditions are specified in the advertisement?

  • Bag is only for the first 200 buyers.
  • Price doesn't include GPRS.
  • You only get chocolate with a minimum 10.000 rupiah refill, and only 4 times. Only effective 14 June–14 August.
