  • Listen to Brendon, Vita and Rizky discuss the differences between fast food in Indonesia and Australia.
  • Make notes in Indonesian on the information provided. It may help to review the Viewing strategies.

(Try to identify the stereotypes or generalisations Rizky and Brendon make about each other’s culture!)
