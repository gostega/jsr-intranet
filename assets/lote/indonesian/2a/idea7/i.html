Indonesian magazines, similar to Australian magazines, have a variety of rubrik. You have seen feature articles, interviews, horoscopes and reviews in the other sections of this course. This topic provides you with the information and opportunity to engage with other sections of magazines, including advertisements and advice letters.

Outcomes

  • You will learn a variety of ways to describe and compare things.
  • You will develop your ability to use persuasive language.
  • You will become aware of some conventions of different Indonesian text types.

You will engage with the following text types:

  • advertisements
  • letters
  • video
  • articles.
