Di-verbs

You will often see di-verbs used in Indonesian. Look at the following example and see if you can work out when the di-verb is used:

  • Teman saya menulis buku itu.
  • Buku itu ditulis oleh teman saya.

When do you think the di-verb is used? Explain in your own words.

There are a number of ways you can explain how to use a di-verb. The best one is the explanation that works for you and allows you to get it right. A simple explanation is:
  • someone does something = me-
  • something is done = di-

Hati-hati!

When something is done by me or you (1st or 2nd person) the di- is dropped and only the base-word is used.
