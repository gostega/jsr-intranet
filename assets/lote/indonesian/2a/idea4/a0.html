Apply what you have learnt from the Discover and Engage sections to complete the following tasks.

Tugas – Penilaian

Find an Indonesian song, or section of film or a television program. There are plenty on the Internet – use your favourite search engine and some key words in Indonesian from the Pojok bahasa.

Choose one of the following options and write approximately 150 words in Indonesian.

  1. Write a review of an Indonesian film extract, television show, music video, song, book, story or article. It doesn’t matter if you don’t get to see the whole film/show. Make it up! Yang penting, bahasa Indonesianya, bukan kebenarannya! Coba kreatif dong!

Textual conventions to consider:

  • structure/set-up of the article
  • expressing personal opinion without using ‘I’
  • use of passive/object focus sentence.
OR
  1. Use the cartoon you viewed earlier, to write a story or narrative account of the incident.

Textual conventions to consider:

  • structure – introductory paragraph, body and conclusion
  • description of characters and setting
  • sequencing of events
  • use of direct speech, quotation marks, and so on.
