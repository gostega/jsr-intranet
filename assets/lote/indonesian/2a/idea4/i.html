Resensi artinya review.

This topic focuses on talking about films. However, you will be given the opportunity to explore and review other Indonesian texts. You’ll find a list of useful terms in the Pojok bahasa (such as lagu Indonesia, sinetron). Use your favourite search engine to look up some of the terms and find other online resources.

Outcomes

  • You will view and make meaning of reviews of films, music and books.
  • You will be able to express your opinion about popular texts, including your likes and dislikes, and give reasons.
  • You will become more confident with object focus sentences.
  • You will learn to employ some conjunctions for extending writing, such as sehingga, supaya, agar, jadi.

You will engage with the following text types:

  • video and film clips
  • reviews of film, music and books
  • a written response to a review
  • informal conversations about films, music and television.
