Questions with object focus

Look at the following question:

Siapa nama bintang film yang paling Anda kagumi?

Klik di sini untuk membaca tentang majalah di Indonesia.

Typically, Anda starts with a capital A, unlike English, where the I is the only pronoun important enough to be capitalised. This reflects a culture of self-effacement (putting others before yourself) that is typical of many Asian cultures. The community is prioritised over the individual, unlike Western culture which prides itself on individualism.

Pertanyaan (Question):
Siapa orang yang paling Anda kagumi?

Jawaban (Answer):
Orang yang paling saya kagumi adalah …

To answer this question correctly you have to:

  • delete the question word Siapa
  • swap I for you
  • add an ‘is’.

Really, it’s not too difficult to respond to object focus questions!
