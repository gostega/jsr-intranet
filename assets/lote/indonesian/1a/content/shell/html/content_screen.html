Introduction

Selamat datang. Welcome to Indonesian Unit 1A. You may have done Indonesian before and be treating this as a refresher course, or you may be new to Indonesian.

This resource contains eight topics which cover the requirements of the unit. These topics are accessible by clicking on the links on the left-hand side.

Each topic has three separate sections which can be accessed by clicking the links at the top of the screen on the topic. These sections are:

  • Introduce
  • Discover & Engage
  • Apply.

The Introduce section covers basic information on the topic, including an explanation of the content, activities and learning outcomes that will be covered.

The Discover & Engage section contains various sections of content which can be accessed via the drop-down menu. It also contains a range of activities that will allow you to put the skills and knowledge you gain into practice.

The Apply section contains assessment tasks covering all aspects of the topic.

You can access the pojok bahasa, pojok budaya or strategi at any time by accessing the link from the left-hand menu.
