Introduce

Going out, especially on malam Minggu, is usually the highlight of the week for anak remaja. In cities in Indonesia, even teenagers who don't have the money to go out find something to do, whether it be nongkrong di mal, minum teh di warung, or cuci mata.

After working through this section, you will be able to ask friends what their plans are in Indonesian, or even ask someone out. You will be able to make arrangements, including place and time, and say how you are getting there.

You will:

  • indicate what you intend to do at a particular time
  • organise to go out with friends
  • specify a day, time and place to meet
  • sequence information
  • read a note, listen to a podcast and view a promotional poster for a mall opening.

Print out the kartu kemajuan to track your progress as you go through this section of work. You should also save it in a folder on your computer.

It should take you approximately seven hours to complete this section.
