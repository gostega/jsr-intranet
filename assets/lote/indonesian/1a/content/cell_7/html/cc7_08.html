Apply

Now apply what you have learned by completing the following tasks.

Complete your kartu kemajuan to see if there is anything you need to revise or seek help with before you work through these tasks.

Tulislah!

In Indonesian, write a note inviting someone to do something with you. You need to state:

  • where you want to go
  • the purpose of the outing
  • when you will go.

For example, 'I want to go to the beach to surf after school on Friday'.

Give a few options so that you can demonstrate that you know how to use link words. You could also include suggestions regarding transportation.

Try to write 50–100 words.

Dengarkanlah!

Bicaralah!

Listen to a phone message and make notes. Then make a phone call yourself to communicate the information requested in the message.

You should also anticipate some other questions you may be asked, or that the person who picks up the phone may not be who you are trying to contact. Prepare for some different scenarios.
