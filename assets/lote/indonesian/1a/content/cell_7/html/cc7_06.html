When you are recounting a story about something that happened on your weekend, you often use words like 'to', 'at' and 'with'. Here are some useful Indonesian words for sharing stories with friends.

bersama with (people)
ke to (a place)
kepada to (a person)
pada at/on/in (for time)
di at/in/on (a place)
untuk for / to do something
