Apply

Now apply what you have learned by completing the following tasks.

Complete your kartu kemajuan to see if there is anything you need to revise or seek help with before you work through these tasks.

Tulislah!

In Indonesian, create a written personal introduction using the language you have learned so far. You may create it in the format of an email, a Word® document, a PowerPoint® presentation or a poster.

You need to consider the following things that will make your language seem more advanced:

  • using a range of different words
  • using link words to extend sentences
  • using Indonesian word order, not transferring English word order (ie noun then owner/adjective.)

Bicaralah!

You will also introduce yourself by speaking Indonesian in a few short sentences. You may do this in person, on the telephone or by recording yourself speaking Indonesian.

You need to consider:

  • speaking fluently (speaking without stops and starts)
  • speaking from memory, not reading out aloud
  • making your Indonesian sound as similar as possible to the Indonesian speakers you have heard (developing your accent).
