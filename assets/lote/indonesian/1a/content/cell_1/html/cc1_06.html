Kamu suka apa?

To say what you like, just say:

Saya suka …

and add the activities or things that you like.

You can also say:
saya kurang suka (kurang means ‘less’)
or
saya tidak begitu suka.

Write the following sentences in good English. When you have had a go, click the Cek jawaban button to see the answer. It is important to realise that in many cases there are lots of different ways of expressing the same thing. Your answer does not have to match perfectly with the answer given. You should be able to see if your answer is practically the same, or if you have made some mistakes.

  1. Saya suka makan es krim dan minum jus jeruk, tetapi saya tidak begitu suka makan sayur-sayuran.

    I like to eat ice cream and drink orange juice, but I don’t really like eating vegetables.

  2. Saya kurang suka berselancar, tetapi saya suka berenang dan berski air.

    I don’t really like surfing, but I like swimming and water-skiing.

  3. Saya suka bermain komputer dan mendengarkan musik.

    I like to play computer and listen to music.

Complete the following sentence:

  1. Saya suka dan tetapi saya tidak begitu suka .

    Did you write something that fits with 'I like ... and ... but I don't like'?


Extend your sentences by using linking words such as dan and tetapi to make your sentence more interesting. You will also find other link words in the kosa kata.
