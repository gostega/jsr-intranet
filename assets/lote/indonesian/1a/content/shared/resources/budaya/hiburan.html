Hiburan di Indonesia

In Indonesia, the types of entertainment available to you depend on where you are and your social status.

Anak remaja dari keluarga kaya yang tinggal di kota besar enjoy many of the same leisure activities as their counterparts in Australia.

Anak remaja dari keluarga miskin have more limited options. Some teenagers have to spend so much time working to stay alive that they have little time to even think about entertainment.

In cities, hiburan gratis includes nongkrong di mal atau di pinggir jalan. People-watching and guitar-playing out on the pavement are popular pastimes all over Indonesia.

Television is probably the most popular form of entertainment in Indonesia, even if a television set is shared by a number of families. However, in isolated villages, where electricity may be limited to a few hours a day, even this option may not be available.

Hiburan tradisional

Before modern entertainment types became widespread, traditional forms of entertainment flourished. These differ from ethnic group to ethnic group.

Traditional dance differs from region to region, and is still a popular hobby for many teenagers, especially girls.

One of the best known traditional art forms of Indonesia is the wayang kulit performance.
