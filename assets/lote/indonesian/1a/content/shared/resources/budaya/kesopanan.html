Kesopanan

Being polite and not expressing extreme feeling is very important in Indonesia.

It is more polite to say 'saya kurang suka' or 'saya tidak begitu suka' than 'saya tidak suka' which sounds rather harsh and impolite. Even when you are responding to 'Apa kabar?', kurang is used instead of tidak to say 'not so good'.
