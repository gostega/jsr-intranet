Verb affixes

Indonesian verbs can be base-words (eg makan, suka), or they can have an affix.

In Indonesian, there are three main prefixes which indicate that a word is a verb. These are:

  • ber-       (eg bermain)
  • me-       (eg menonton)
  • di-         (eg ditonton)

This is helpful to know because it can help you guess the meaning of the sentence.

For example:

Saya suka berbelanja.

If you don't know the word berbelanja, you can:

a) guess that it's an action, not a noun

b) recognise the ber- prefix, take it off the word, and find belanja in your dictionary.
