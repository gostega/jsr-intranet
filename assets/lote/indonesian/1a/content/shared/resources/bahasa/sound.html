The sound of Indonesian

Alphabet

Bahasa Indonesia is spelled phonetically. The alphabet is the same as English, but the pronunciation is slightly different.

Click on any of the letters below to hear the letter sound. You should repeat each letter aloud, mimicking the pronunciation as closely as possible.
