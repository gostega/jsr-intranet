Introduce

Gaya dan tren are an important part of dunia remaja. There are numerous styles and trends, both mainstream and alternative. Identity can be expressed through what you choose to wear, buy, listen to and watch – or choose not to. One of the ways you define yourself is by what you choose to consume.

In this section, you will explore the fusion of fashion and identity in Indonesia di dunia remaja. You will build on what you have learned about describing people by discussing their fashion choices, and learn a bit more about gaya punk dan tren lain. The language you learn in this unit will enable you to berbelanja di Indonesia.

You will:

  • view and respond to articles on music, fashion and identity
  • learn about shopping culture and the language needed to buy things in Indonesia
  • view a clothing catalogue and discuss with someone what you would like to buy
  • listen to a phone conversation and respond
  • read an online web-forum for skaters
  • view a video clip and some advertisements.

Print out the kartu kemajuan to track your progress as you go through this section of work. You should also save it in a folder on your computer.

It should take you approximately eight hours to complete this section.
