Musik dan fesyen

Gaya dan tren can often be seen in the music world. There is a strong relationship between music and fashion.

Watch a music video clip (for example, Punk Hari Ini by Superman is Dead) or look at the excerpt Punk: 30 Years of Rebellion (Reproduced with the permission of Hai magazine). Read through the following common features of language that you may come across in songs or magazine articles.

Kata disingkatkan

There are a few ways of shortening words. Sometimes you will see a word that looks like the abbreviation of another word – it often is what you think it is, but it helps to know some of the common ways of shortening words.

You already know that you can drop the me- prefix to shorten words in casual conversations.

  • Lagu Superman is Dead
  • Artikel Punk
  • -kah?

-kah is a suffix which makes a word a definite question. It can be used with question words.

di manakah?

siapakah?

apakah?

It can also make non-question words into questions:

Maukah kamu ke pantai?

Inginkah Anda beli sesuatu?

Before you go to your dictionary to find out the meaning of a word, you also need to be aware of words that have been adapted from English. Often you can guess them as the spelling is so similar (anarki, simpel), but sometimes it helps to say a word out loud as the spelling is less obvious (eg imej). You should also always look at the base-word, as often Indonesian affixes like me-kan or ber- are added to English-based words.
