Mau beli apa?

The following phrases are frequently heard by the buyers and the sellers.

The phrases on the right hand side are responses to the questions, but they have been shuffled.

Without knowing what they mean, can you match the response on the right to the question?

Even without knowing the meaning of the phrases, you can use key words from the question to build a response. Check the kosa kata for the meaning of the phrases. Try to learn them so you can use them later on.
