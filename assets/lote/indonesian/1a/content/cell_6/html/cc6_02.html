Subkultur

Ada berbagai subkultur yang berkaitan to dengan tren musik. Dari punk sampai Indie pop, dari reggae sampai hip-hop dan J-pop (yaitu Japan pop), ada banyak sekali fans di Indonesia yang suka meniru gaya idolanya. Ada anak punk, ada anak indie pop, dan sebagainya.

While anak translates as child, it is not as 'childish' sounding as in English. If someone called you a child, you would probably feel insulted. While the word remaja is used in Indonesia, anak has broader meanings than just child, and it does not have the same 'lowering' connotation. For example, people may call themselves anak Indonesia, or anak Yogya, or anak punk.
