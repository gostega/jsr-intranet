Hobi dan obsesi

On her biodata, Cinta Mawar Lestari lists her hobi dan obsesi as:

  • membaca
  • menari
  • menonton
  • menjadi bintang film Hollywood.

All the verbs used in this section of her biodata begin with the prefix me-. Me- is a prefix you will see used a lot. However, you will not find the English translation of the word under me- in the dictionary, as words are listed under their base-words.

Work through the Me- verbs section in the pojok bahasa, tata bahasa.

You probably noticed that the last two words had -kan suffixes as well as the me- prefix. Indonesian suffixes do not change the base-word at all, so are easy to remove as long as you recognise them. Verb suffixes include -i and -kan, although teenagers sometime substitute -in for -kan.
