Apa atau siapa?

Question words are very important in Indonesian.

If you are asking a question about an object or thing, you use the word apa. For example:
Apa makanan favorit Anda? / Apa makanan favoritmu?

If you are asking a question about a name of a person, you use the word siapa. For example:
Siapa penyanyi favorit Anda? / Siapa penyanyi favoritmu?

Remember: Anda (or saya) is used in more formal contexts or with people you don't know well, and kamu/mu (or aku) is used for closer relationships or informal contexts.

Did you notice that the object being asked about in two of the three siapa questions started with the prefix pe-? Pe- can be added to a number of words to make them the person or thing who does the base-word. For example:

penyanyi = person who sings (nyanyi = sing)
pemain = person who plays (main = play).
