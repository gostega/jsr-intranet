Apply

Now apply what you have learned by completing the following tasks.

Complete your kartu kemajuan to see if there is anything you need to revise or seek help with before you work through these tasks.

Tulislah!

Using the skills and knowledge you have gained in Indonesian so far, and the model biodata (female / male) provided, complete one of the following tasks:

  • If you are just beginning Indonesian and feel you are not able to write whole sentences and paragraphs, create your own personal biodata in Indonesian in table form.
  • If you feel ready to have a go at writing full sentences and paragraphs, write approximately half a page about yourself in Indonesian, including the information you would put in a biodata.

Things to consider when writing include:

  • using extended sentences with link words
  • taking risks with language to experiment with new words
  • taking risks by manipulating sentence structures
  • extending writing to include unpractised topics
  • the form of ‘I’ or ‘you’ you use.

Bacalah!

Find an Indonesian language biodata. You can find one online by searching key words such as ‘biodata selebriti indonesia’, in a magazine, or your teacher may provide one.

Create an English language version of the biodata, making sure you include the main points and elaborating on supporting information if you can.
