The next section of the biodata (female / male) is ukuran.

Apa artinya ukuran?

If you don’t know the meaning of a word, there are various ways to ask. One way to ask is to say ‘Apa artinya…?’

For example:
Apa artinya ukuran?
Ukuran artinya ‘measurements’.

Memorise the repair strategies given in the strategi section.

Ukurannya berapa?

Berapa is a question word related to numbers. It can go at the beginning or the end of a question.

Question:
Ukuran celananya berapa?
Berapa ukuran celananya?

Answer:
Ukurannya 26.
