In Indonesian, introduce yourself verbally, using all the information you have just filled in.

To do this, just add a personal pronoun for ‘my’. You can use saya or ku (depending on how formal or informal you wish to be) after the words in the first column on the form. For example:

Nama lengkap saya … (whatever your name is).

You can also use your name instead of saya/aku for the tempat dan tanggal lahir.

For more details on personal pronouns, go to:

  • Personal pronouns

If you need to review numbers or learn the months in Indonesian, go to:

  • The sound of Indonesian

Unlike the English ‘I’, saya/aku/gue are not capitalised. However, the word Anda (you) always starts with a capital letter.

Names can also be used for ‘you’ or ‘I’.
