Introduce

When you want to get to know someone, there are a lot of things you may want to find out. Different cultures consider different things important when getting to know people, whether in personal introductions or written information, such as celebrity profiles. In this section you will develop the language skills to understand Indonesian biodata by exploring two celebrity biodata. You will also develop your understanding of things that are considered important in Indonesian introductions.

This section covers a range of language related to the sections of biodata.

You will:

  • search for some Indonesian biodata online
  • summarise or reproduce in English an Indonesian biodata
  • produce your own personal biodata in Indonesian.

Print out the kartu kemajuan to track your progress as you go through this section of work. You should also save it in a folder on your computer.

It should take you approximately seven hours to complete this section.
