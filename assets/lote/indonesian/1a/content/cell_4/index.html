Introduce

Now you have learnt the basics, it is time to extend yourself by engaging in some longer and less predictable conversations. There is still some predictability about what you can expect to be asked in conversation. This section will help you guess the meaning of questions, clarify things you are uncertain of, and respond appropriately. You will learn how to initiate conversation and engage others in discussion. You will move from observing conversations to participating in them. At the end of the section, you will be interviewed for an Indonesian teen magazine article on pelajar asing bahasa Indonesia.

You will:

  • open conversations, greet people and ask how they are
  • view and listen to a range of introductory conversations
  • ask and answer questions about ability, wants and likes
  • read and respond to a magazine interview with a celebrity.

Print out the kartu kemajuan to track your progress as you go through this section of work. You should also save it in a folder on your computer.

It should take you approximately six hours to complete this section.
