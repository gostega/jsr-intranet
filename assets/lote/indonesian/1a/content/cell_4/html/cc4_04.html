Mengapa means why.

Berapa lama?

Audio CD – Track 17

Please upgrade your flash player. You can get the latest version here: http://www.adobe.com/flashplayer/

Berapa, as you already know, asks for a number.

'Berapa lama?' means 'How long?'

The response usually starts with sudah or baru.

Sudah dua tahun.

Baru dua tahun.

Write the answers to the following questions and send them to your teacher.

  1. Apa hobi favorit Anda?
  2. Sudah berapa lama Anda melakukan hobi itu?
  3. Berapa lama Anda belajar bahasa Indonesia?
  4. Anda tinggal di mana?
  5. Sudah berapa lama Anda tinggal di sana?
