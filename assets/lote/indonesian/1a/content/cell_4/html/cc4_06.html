Tulislah!

Create an alternative presentation about one of the idols, using English and/or images to demonstrate your understanding of the Indonesian language used to describe them. You can present it in whatever form you like, for example as a report, story, email, profile or other. The focus is not on the format or presentation but on your understanding of Indonesian.

To demonstrate a deeper understanding, you can include information that is inferred rather than directly stated in the interview (for example religion, self-image).

When you have finished, complete the worksheet to submit with your presentation.
