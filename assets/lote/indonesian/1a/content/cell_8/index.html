Introduce

Dunia remaja should perhaps be renamed dunia-dunia remaja, as your world varies greatly depending on who you are and where you live. Anak Indonesia have a different world to anak Australia, anak desa have a different world to anak kota, and anak miskin live very different lives to anak kaya. Are there any common factors in dunia remaja?

In this section, you will explore the diversity of dunia remaja. You will consolidate the language you have learned so far, and be able to draw comparisons between your world and the worlds of Indonesian teenagers.

You will:

  • consider what is important to you as a teenager and to Indonesian teenagers
  • consider the different influences on dunia remaja
  • make comparisons between dunia remaja Australia and dunia remaja Indonesia
  • write about your world.

Print out the kartu kemajuan to track your progress as you go through this section of work. You should also save it in a folder on your computer.

It should take you approximately eight hours to complete this section.
