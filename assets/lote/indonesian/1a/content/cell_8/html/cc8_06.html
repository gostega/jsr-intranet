As part of his KKN (university field work), Suci's brother Lukman has gone to do some work in a village. Click on the link under the postcard to flip it over and read what he has written.

Text alternative

Kepada Suci yang baik,

Hari ini (hari Sabtu) saya berjalan ke desa teman saya yang tidak jauh dari Wonogiri, Jawa Tengah. Rumah di desa sederhana sekali. Apalagi hiburannya. Tidak ada komputer. HP saya tidak ada sinyal. Lain sekali dengan kota. Di desa ini, ada satu televisi antara tujuh keluarga. Setiap hari pada jam tujuh malam, orang berkumpul di dekat televisi untuk menonton televisi. Semuanya minum teh dan nonton televisi sambil bercakap-cakap. Orang yang tidak mau menonton televisi duduk-duduk, bermain gitar, menyanyi dan bercakap-cakap. Saya akan menginap di sini selama tiga minggu. Wah, tiga minggu tanpa komputer, HP atau teknologi modern yang lain. Wah, gawat! Tapi pemandangan indah sekali. Ada gunung, ada padi, ada kerbau dan monyet. Tidak ada mobil atau sepeda motor. Tidak ada polusi. Saya merasa tenang di sini.

Salam,

Lukman

Summarise the postcard in English.

Here is a translation of Lukman's postcard. Read it and then answer the questions below.

Dear Suci

Today (Saturday) I went to my friend's village, which is not far from Wonogiri in Central Java. The houses in the village are very simple. So is the entertainment! There are no computers. My mobile has no signal. Very different to the city. In this village, there is one television between seven families. Every day at seven pm, people gather near the television to watch. They all drink tea and watch TV while talking. People who don't want to watch television sit around playing guitar, singing and talking. I will stay there for three weeks. Wow, three weeks without a computer, mobile or other modern technology. Difficult! But the views are beautiful. There are mountains, rice fields and buffaloes and monkeys. There are no cars or motorbikes. No pollution. I feel at peace here.

Love

Lukman
