Pertama

Before you begin to work through this section, brainstorm, alone or in a group, what the factors are that make up dunia remaja.

Break up your brainstorm into three different sections, using the Y chart provided. Use the following categories.

  • Kelihatan (looks like)
  • Kedengaran (sounds like)
  • Perasaan (feels like).

You may think of some other things that do not fit. Add them as well.
