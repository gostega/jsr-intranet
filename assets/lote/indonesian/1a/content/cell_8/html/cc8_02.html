Brack's lecturer has asked him to compare two similar Indonesian websites. Use the comparing and contrasting words in the kosa kata to interpret the sentences he has written.

The statements may be true or false, depending on what sites you have looked at.

In the box below each sentence type what you think Brack's sentence means, and click 'Cek jawaban' when you have finished.

  1. Situs cowok berwarna hitam sedangkan situs cewek berwarna merah muda.

    The boys' site is black, whereas the girls' site is pink.

  2. Situs cewek ada musik, sedangkan situs cowok tidak ada musik.

    The girls' site has music, whereas the boys' site doesn't have music.

  3. Akan tetapi, situs cowok lebih menarik daripada situs cewek.

    However, the boys' site is more interesting than the girls' site.

  4. Situs cewek tentang pacaran dan dandanan akan tetapi situs cowok tentang musik, hobi dan feysen.

    The girls' site is about boyfriends and makeup; however, the boys' site is about music, hobbies and fashion.

  5. Situs cowok dan situs cewek sangat berbeda.

    The boys' site and the girls' site are very different.


Try writing five of your own sentences comparing the websites you have looked at. You may make up your own sentences or you may use Brack's (or parts of Brack's) as a model.
