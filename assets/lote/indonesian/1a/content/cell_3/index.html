Introduce

Diri saya means 'myself'. This section extends the topic of introductions to talking about yourself. The emphasis is on what Indonesians consider to be important when defining themselves. This will enable you to introduce yourself in a culturally appropriate way, and help you understand a range of language used in introductions, personal conversations and other texts, such as a vodcast for a concert tour, and an annotated holiday photo album.
