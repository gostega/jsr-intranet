Bagaimana mukanya?

Roll your mouse over each part of the face to learn the words.

Text alternative

The Indonesian word for face is muka and the following are the words for the parts of the face.

eyes mata skin kulit
nose hidung cheeks pipi
lips bibir chin dagu
eyelashes bulu mata eyebrows alis mata

 

Read the following description, noting the word order and the use of the -nya suffix for his/hers.

Badan dia langsing, kulitnya sawo matang.

Hidungnya pesek, bibirnya tebal, matanya bulat, bulu matanya lentik.
