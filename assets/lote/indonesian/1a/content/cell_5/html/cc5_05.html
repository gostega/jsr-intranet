Apa/Ada?

Apa is a question word meaning 'what'?

Ada means 'there is' / 'there are', or can mean 'is there?' / 'are there?' with the right tone of voice.

Look at the difference in meaning between the two following sentences:

Apa yang dapat menyatukan kita?

Ada yang dapat menyatukan kita?

Tip: You could substitute nationalities for the ethnic groups, subcultures (eg skaters), or even adjectives related to personality (eg lucu). You may have some other ideas yourself – don't limit yourself to just these ideas. Remember, you can always check the pojok bahasa for inspiration.
