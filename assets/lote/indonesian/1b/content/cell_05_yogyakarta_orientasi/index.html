Trish and Daniel travel to Yogyakarta, a city considered by many to be the 'cultural heart' of Indonesia. Here you will learn the basics of coping with everyday life in Indonesia, including what to expect in a home-stay and eating out.

The tantangan Trish and Daniel receive in Yogya involves food. You will learn about the different options for eating out and how to order food. You will also learn how to ask for and follow directions, which will help you find your way around.

At the end of this section, you will consolidate your skills by listening to a conversation and discussing the information provided in this section with someone else. You will also read and respond to a piece of fan-mail Daniel and Trish receive from an Indonesian fan.

You will:

  • listen to a range of conversations to practise your listening skills and learn new vocabulary and phrases
  • revise language related to travel and going out
  • learn language related to home-stay in Indonesia
  • ask questions and make comparisons
  • understand, follow and give directions
  • develop your understanding of eating and food in Indonesia.

Print out the kartu kemajuan to track your progress as you go through this section of work. You should also save it in a folder on your computer.

It should take you approximately nine hours to complete this section.
