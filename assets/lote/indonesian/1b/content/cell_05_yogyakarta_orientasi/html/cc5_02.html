Tulislah!

Practise using some of the new vocabulary by writing a full description of your room in Indonesian for an Indonesian penpal.

Remember, you can use prepositions (location words such as di atas, di bawah, di samping) and adjectives to make your description more interesting. The following phrases may also be useful:

  • Di kamar saya ada
  • dengan
  • yang berwarna …
  • yang (adjective).
