Pertanyaan dan permohonan

Review the question words in the kosa kata and look at the following request words.

  • Bolehkah saya?
  • Ada(kah)?
  • Haruskah saya?
  • Di mana?
  • Mengapa?
  • Yang mana?
  • Mohon

Comparisons

To compare two items, you can use the phrase: … lebih (adjective) daripada …

  • Daniel lebih tinggi daripada Trish.
  • Rambut Trish lebih panjang daripada rambut Daniel.

Bicaralah!

Put yourself in Trish and Daniel's place. Think of some questions you might ask if you were staying with a family in Indonesia. For example:

Bolehkah saya pinjam handuk?

Adakah tissue untuk kamar kecil?

Make a list of five questions you may need to ask. Then role-play asking someone the questions.
