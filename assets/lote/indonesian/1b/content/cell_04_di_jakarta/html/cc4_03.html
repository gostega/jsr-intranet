Jam berapa?

Now is a good time to review your numbers and telling the time. You already know that time on the hour is just jam or pukul and then the number (satu/dua/tiga).

You have also learned the words for plus (lewat) or minus (kurang). For example:

3:15 Jam tiga lewat limabelas
3:45 Jam empat kurang limabelas (four o'clock minus fifteen minutes).

Jam setengah …

Instead of saying 'half past', Indonesians frequently say 'half to (the next hour)'. For example:

3:30 half before four: jam setengah empat.
12:30 is jam setengah satu.

Do not use the 24-hour system when saying the time.
