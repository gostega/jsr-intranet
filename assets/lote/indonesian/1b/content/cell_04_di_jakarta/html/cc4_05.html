Tantangan mini

The kontestan have been paired up for their tantangan mini, and set up with a camera crew to help them find their way around. Trish and Daniel have received the following challenge. They have one day to complete it.

Selamat! Inilah tantangan pertama. Kalian harus menjadi pemandu wisata untuk turis domestik di Jakarta. Carilah tiga tempat wisata yang menarik dan perkenalkanlah tempat-tempat itu kepada para pemirsa. Inilah tempat-tempat yang harus Anda cari:

  1. Tempat di mana seluruh Indonesia dapat dilihat di satu tempat
  2. Tempat yang menarik bagi remaja
  3. Tempat bersejarah.

pemandu = guide   wisata = tourist   (pemandu wisata = tour guide)

para pemirsa = TV audience


In the note, find:

  • a greeting

    selamat

  • an instruction word

    carilah

  • a word for 'you' plural

    kalian

Who is the intended audience of Trish and Daniel's presentation?

Turis domestik / domestic tourists are mentioned. They are also asked to find somewhere of interest to teenagers, so this could be the intended audience.

What three places do Trish and Daniel have to find? Explain in English.

  1. A place where there is the whole of Indonesia in one place
  2. A place that is interesting for teenagers
  3. A historical place.

Cari informasi

Which places in Jakarta could Daniel and Trish film to fulfil their tantangan? Find places on the web that fit these clues. Start by looking at the website: www.jakarta-tourism.go.id/?lang=ina

See if you can work with the site in Indonesian first – you will be surprised at what you can understand – then swap to English when you need to.

Now focus on the finding tourist attractions on the site.

Use the worksheet to categorise the places listed on the website as:

  • Tempat yang menarik bagi remaja
  • Tempat bersejarah
  • Tempat wisata budaya
  • Lain-lain.

Find some other places not listed on this website that fit into the categories.

Then write three sentences in Indonesian saying where you want to go and why. You can use the following pattern:

Saya mau mengunjungi … karena …
