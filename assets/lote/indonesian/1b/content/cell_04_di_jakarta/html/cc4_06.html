The Jakartanese speak notoriously fast – you may find all native speakers fast, but Jakartans seem to talk in hyperspeed! Listen for the phrase Daniel uses to ask for the speaker to repeat what she has said slowly.

Also note how he politely begins a conversation with a stranger.

What formal words or phrases tell you that the situation is formal and polite? What words or phrases communicate respect for the person being addressed?

permisi
addressing the woman as Ibu
asking 'bolehkah saya bertanya' / asking permission to ask a question
using saya

Research Taman Mini on the web. Find out as much as you can. Try exploring a Indonesian Taman Mini site and see what you can understand.

Plan to spend a couple of hours at Taman Mini. Write yourself a brief itinerary. What would you do/see? What would it cost?

What words would you use to describe things at Taman Mini? Here are some to get you started.

  • murah
  • mahal
  • indah
  • menyenangkan
  • membosankan
  • menarik

You can use the following words to modify adjectives.

  • sangat menarik
  • agak mahal
  • kurang baik
  • cukup murah

These words are also in the kosa kata. You can save the kosa kata document to your computer and add some other adjectives you find useful.
