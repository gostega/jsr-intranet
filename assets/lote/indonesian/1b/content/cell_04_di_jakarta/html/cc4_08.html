Write five sentences of your own describing the location of sites you choose in Jakarta.

You may have noticed the word yang used in this section. Yang is very useful in describing things. It is often used before an adjective, for example:

tempat yang menarik

orang yang ramah

It can also be used in describing location:

obyek wisata yang terletak di sebelah utara kota

Bicaralah!
