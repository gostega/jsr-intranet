Conversations

Indonesians are very friendly. As a tourist in Indonesia, you can expect to be approached by people wanting to talk to you, practise their English, or even take their photo with you. It is quite normal for a total stranger to walk up, greet you, introduce themselves, then proceed to ask your name, where you are from, where you are going, where you have just been, how old you are, whether you have a girl/boyfriend, if you are married, how much you earn … and so on. It is seen as being friendly, not nosey, so don't be offended!
