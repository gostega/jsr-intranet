Garuda

Reproduced with the
permission of Lisa Djanegara

The garuda is a mythical bird from Hindu and Buddhist mythology, something like an eagle or the legendary phoenix. It features highly in Indonesian national symbols. The national coat of arms consists of a garuda, the Pancasila (five principles) and the national motto Bhinneka Tunggal Ika.

Garuda is of course also the name of Indonesia's national airline. The promotional song and video, Kebangaanku, was released in 2008. As well as the Garuda symbol, the clip draws on a number of other nationally recognised symbols or ideas, including the Sang Saka Merah Putih and the unofficial national motto of kerjasama.
