Many Indonesian place names are named after Indonesian heroes, especially military or historic figures. Jakarta's Soekarno-Hatta airport is named after the first president (Soekarno) and vice-president (Mohammad Hatta) of Indonesia. Yogyakarta's Adisucipto airport is named after Agustinus Adisutjipto, a commodore in the Indonesian air force who made the first airflight in independent Indonesia on 10 October 1945.

Jenderal Sudirman was the commander of the Indonesian armed forces during the fight for Independence in the 1940s. There is a Jalan Jenderal Sudirman in Jakarta, in Yogyakarta and in many other cities in Indonesia. You will see numerous other street names that salute Indonesia's military history, such as Jalan Jenderal Ahmad Yani, Jalan Letjen Suprapto, Jalan Mayjen Sungkono, and others. These people are generally heroes from the war of Independence or one of the seven generals killed in the 1965 (alleged) Communist coup.
