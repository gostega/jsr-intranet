Introduction

Selamat datang. Indonesian Unit 1B, Berjalan-jalan di Indonesia, focuses on travel to and around Indonesia. You will follow the story of Daniel and Trish, two Australian teenagers who are selected to take part in a reality television show Tantangan Indonesia (Indonesia Challenge). The show involves them travelling to Indonesia and taking part in a number of challenges in different parts of the country. Along with Daniel and Trish, you will gain the language skills and knowledge to help you prepare for a trip, understand some challenging texts (such as announcements, magazine articles and websites), negotiate your way around and ask for help.

This resource contains seven topics which cover the requirements of the unit. These topics include:

  • Diterima – Filling out forms, understanding the information pack
  • Persiapan – Asking people for advice about travelling to Indonesia
  • Perjalanan ke Indonesia – On the plane
  • Di Jakarta – Finding your way around the capital
  • Yogyakarta: Orientasi – What to expect at a home-stay in Indonesia and eating out
  • Tantangan Lombok – Shopping for food and cooking on Lombok
  • Tantangan utama – Endangered Indonesian animals

Each topic begins with an introduction which explains the content, activities and learning outcomes. Within each topic, there are two sections which can be accessed by clicking on the tabs at the top of the screen.

These sections are:

  • Discover and engage
  • Apply.

The Discover and engage section contains various subsections which can be accessed via the dropdown menu. These subsections contain a range of activities that will allow you to put the skills and knowledge into practice.

The Apply section contains assessment tasks relevant to the topic.

You can access the pojok bahasa, pojok budaya or strategi at any time by accessing the link from the left-hand menu.

When you are ready, select a topic on the left-hand side to begin.
