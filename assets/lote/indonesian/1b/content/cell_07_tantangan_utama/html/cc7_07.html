Laporan

You are taking Daniel or Trish's place on Tantangan Indonesia. Present your final report on an endangered Indonesian animal. You could use footage of the animal from the internet, or, if you are living somewhere where you can visit a wildlife park, you could video your report live. (If you are doing a 'live' report and the Indonesian animals listed are not a possibility, discuss other options with your teacher.)

Your teacher may ask you to present the report in a specific format (for example, spoken commentary or telephone interview) but it should contain the following information:

  • Binatang ini terdapat di mana?
  • Berapa kira-kira jumlah binatang ini yang masih tersisa di alam liar?
  • Apa ancaman terhadap binatang ini?
  • Apa yang binatang ini lakukan?
  • Apa yang dimakan binatang itu?
  • Dia makan berapa kali sehari/seminggu?

Dengarkanlah!
