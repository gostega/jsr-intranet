Choose one of the satwa Indonesia terancam punah to report on.

Find a precise location (such as a taman nasional or daerah) where the animal can be seen. Plan a trip to this location. Work out:

  • how you will get there
  • approximately how long the journey will take
  • where you will stay.

Tulislah!

In Indonesian, write a blog entry on your trip. You have viewed a number of blog entries by Daniel and Trish describing your journey. You may wish to review these for ideas on sentence structure and some useful words.
