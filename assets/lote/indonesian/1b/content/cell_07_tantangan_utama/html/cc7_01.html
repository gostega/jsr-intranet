Tantangan utama

Anda harus pergi ke daerah yang belum Anda kunjungi dan membuat laporan tentang salah satu binatang/satwa animal/fauna Indonesia yang terancam punah. Pilihlah salah satu dari binatang-binatang berikut:

orang utan / komodo / harimau / macan tutul / anoa / macan dahan / badak Sumatera / beruang madu.

Satwa atau binatang?

The word for animal is binatang, but satwa is also used, especially in the context of conservation, like the word 'fauna' in English.

The first step is to find out where these animals can be found. You can find some information at http://id.wikipedia.org, but you may also need to search elsewhere. The following links are helpful:

  • http://cegahsatwapunah.mybuckster.com/
  • http://www.lablink.or.id/index.html (environment and development link, then go to satwa page – click on orang utan picture).
  • http://www.wwf.or.id go to 'species'
  • http://www.animalinfo.org/ check the country index
  • http://beruangmadu.org/
  • http://dotsub.com/demo.jsp/
  • http://www.badak.or.id/
