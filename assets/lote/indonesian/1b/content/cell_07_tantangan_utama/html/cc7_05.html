Satwa yang terancam punah

Daniel has started drafting his report. Read his notes and try to guess which animal he is researching.

Text alternative

Binatang ini jarang terlihat. Dia terdapat di hutan di Sumatera.

Dia bisa berenang dan kadang-kadang berenang kalau cuaca panas.

Dia sering tidur dan hanya makan daging.

Binatang ini tidak pernah makan tumbuh-tumbuhan.

jarang = rarely tidak pernah = never
kadang2 = sometimes,
occasionally 		hanya = only
sering = often bisa = can

Which animal do you think Daniel is writing his report on? Explain your reasons.

It is an animal that is rarely seen.

It sometimes swims.

It often sleeps and only eats meat.
