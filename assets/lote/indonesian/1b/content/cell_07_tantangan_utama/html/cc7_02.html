Use the suggested reading strategies to help you make sense of key information from the page Trish found.

  1. Read the following excerpt and highlight the words for mammals, birds and fish, and any information associated with them.
  2. Also find any variations of the word 'kaya' (ie kaya with affixes).

'Indonesia sangat kaya akan flora dan fauna, sehingga Indonesia dikenal sebagai salah satu negara terkaya akan keanekaragaman hayati … Indonesia nomor satu di dunia memiliki kekayaan jenis mamalia nomer satu di dunia (515 jenis), dan nomor empat untuk kekayaan burung (lebih 1539 jenis). Untuk ikan, sekitar 45% ikan di dunia itu berada di perairan Indonesia.'

From http://www.p-wec.com/content/id/program_edukasi.html

Use the information you have found to answer the questions below.

  1. You should have highlighted the words used for various types of fauna mentioned in the article. In the text box, type the information provided about:

    • mammals

      Indonesia has the most variety of any country in the world (515 types).

    • birds

      Indonesia has the fourth highest number of birds (1539 species).

    • fish

      Indonesia has 45% of the world's fish species in its waters.

  2. From your knowledge of Indonesian grammar so far, if kaya means 'rich', guess the meaning of the following words. Type what you think the words mean in the text box, and see if you were right by clicking cek jawaban:

    • kekayaan

      richness/wealth

    • terkaya

      most rich


Now read the following segment on satwa liar yang terancam punah from Profauna's website (see the link below). Use similar strategies (looking for words that are similar to English, looking for key words and numbers) to identify key information.


Fakta tentang satwa Indonesia

Diperkirakan sebanyak 300.000 jenis satwa liar atau sekitar 17% satwa di dunia terdapat di Indonesia … Meskipun kaya, namun Indonesia dikenal juga sebagai negara yang memiliki daftar panjang tentang satwa liar yang terancam punah. Saat ini jumlah jenis satwa liar Indonesia yang terancam punah adalah 147 jenis mamalia, 114 jenis burung, 28 jenis reptil, 91 jenis ikan dan 28 jenis invertebrata (IUCN, 2003). Satwa-satwa tersebut benar-benar akan punah dari alam jika tidak ada tindakan untuk menyelamatkanya.

From http://www.profauna.or.id/Indo/Fakta_satwa.html


In English, summarise the information given about the number and type of endangered species in Indonesia.

147 species of mammal, 114 species of bird, 28 species of reptile, 91 species of fish and 28 species of invertebrate
