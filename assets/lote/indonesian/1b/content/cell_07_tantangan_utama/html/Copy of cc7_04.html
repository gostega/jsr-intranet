Sedang apa?

Trish is planning her presentation. She wants some live-action shots of animals with a documentary-style commentary on what they are doing. To do this, verbs and the word sedang are needed.

Brainstorm a list of actions (verbs) that you may need to describe an animal's activity while you are filming it (for example, sleeping). You may do this in Indonesian and English, looking up any words you think you may need.

When you have thought of as many verbs as you can, click here.
