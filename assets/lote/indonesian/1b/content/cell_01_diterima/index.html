Diterima

Daniel and Trish have been selected to take part in a reality television show called Tantangan Indonesia which is being produced by TV Rubahtel Indonesia. The show features foreigners doing a scavenger hunt around Indonesia while filming their travels. Daniel and Trish know they will be travelling to areas where they cannot rely on English being spoken, so they have to improve their Indonesian.

In this section you will help Daniel and Trish make sense of their information pack, and you will get an idea of things you need to do and consider before planning a trip to Indonesia. Doing this will develop your reading skills. You will also start to develop an interactive map to track both your learning and the journey you take through Indonesia with Daniel and Trish.

You will:

  • read and make sense of the information pack for Tantangan Indonesia
  • listen in on Daniel's conversation with the Consulate staff member and identify the information needed to fill in his visa forms
  • fill in your own visa forms for a trip to Indonesia
  • read the list of things to take to Indonesia and a basic itinerary
  • understand how affixes can be removed to find the meaning of words
  • revise and apply a range of reading strategies.

Print out the kartu kemajuan to track your progress as you go through this section of work. You should also save it in a folder on your computer.

It should take you approximately eight hours to complete this section.
