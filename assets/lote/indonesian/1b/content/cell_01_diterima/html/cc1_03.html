Awalan dan akhiran

In doing the highlighting in the daftar barang bawaan, you may have noticed that over 20 per cent of the words have affixes. This shows how important they are in Indonesian. You need to recognise affixes so you can find the meaning of words in the dictionary, as they are listed under their base-word (ie without the affix).

Type the base-word in the box provided using lowercase letters to find out the meaning of the words below.

You can see from the table above that sometimes there is a very close link between the base-word and the affixed word, and sometimes there isn't. Knowledge of grammar can help you make sense of words with affixes without going to your dictionary. For example, can you guess the meaning of the following affixed words given the meaning of the base-word?
