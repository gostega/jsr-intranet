Rencana

Trish has her laptop on the plane. She knows they will start in Jakarta and then move on to Yogyakarta, but it is a bit of a mystery after that. Where would you like to go in Indonesia?

Use the interactive map of Indonesia you have seen in the previous topic and the map of Jakarta from the Garuda in-flight magazine to explore some interesting places.

Make some notes on your own interactive map to plot:

  • where you think you will go
  • what you will do/see there
  • how long you will spend in each place.

Then write a paragraph in Indonesian explaining where you would go in a two-week period. Practise using some of the following phrases to build your paragraph.

  • saya mau ke
  • naik
  • lalu
  • kemudian
  • sesudah itu
  • setelah itu
  • sebelum ke
  • pada hari Senin
  • pada hari berikutnya
  • selama dua hari
  • saya akan tinggal di sana

You will find a list of link words in the kosa kata. You will need to keep using these and adding to them
