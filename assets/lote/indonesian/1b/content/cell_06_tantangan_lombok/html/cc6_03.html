Membaca resep

Recipes contain very specific language. There are words for quantities, food words, classifiers, instructions words (verbs) and so on. It would take a long time to learn all the words you would need to read any recipe in Indonesian, so a useful strategy is to identify common words and learn those.

Bacalah!

Check out http://www.eresep.com/ and choose five Indonesian savoury recipes. You may print them off or copy and paste them into a document. Without worrying about understanding all of the words, use different coloured highlighters or symbols to identify the following:

  • words for quantities (eg gram, cup, teaspoon)
  • words for ingredients
  • verbs or action words (remember the verb prefixes to look for – check the pojok bahasa section if you have forgotten).

Once you have done this, again without worrying what the words mean, identify words for quantities, ingredients and actions that are repeated a few times.

You will find some common cooking terms listed in the kosa kata section.
