Tantangan Lombok

Reproduced with the permission
of Bree Sonter

Daniel and Trish's next challenge is travelling to the island paradise of Lombok and gaining a more in-depth understanding of Indonesian cooking. You will use your reading and viewing skills in this unit to interpret the challenge, read Trish's blog entry, and watch some of the video footage Daniel and Trish create to complete their tantangan. You will also learn how to read, interpret and write recipes and will be able to explain how to make something yourself. At the end of the section, you will read a text related to food and demonstrate your understanding.

You will:

  • read a blog entry and the latest tantangan instructions
  • listen to conversations in the market
  • learn to recognise common classifiers, quantities, ingredients and instructions to be able to follow a recipe
  • learn about bargaining for food and practise bargaining yourself
  • observe some Indonesian foods being made
  • write recipes in Indonesian
  • demonstrate how to cook something yourself, explaining the process in Indonesian.

Print out the kartu kemajuan to track your progress as you go through this section of work. You should also save it in a folder on your computer.

It should take you approximately seven hours to complete this section.
