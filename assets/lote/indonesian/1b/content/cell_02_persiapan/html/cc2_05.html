Summarise what you understand of Budi's advice to Trish and Daniel re clothing.

What you should wear depends where you go in Indonesia. You don't need a sweater. You can wear shorts and t shirt in Bali or Jakarta, but elsewhere you should wear suitable clothing. It is better if you wear formal clothing. No miniskirts or sleeveless tops.
