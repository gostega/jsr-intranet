Pikirkanlah

Revise question words. Print the table of question words and pin it somewhere in your workspace so you can refer to it frequently until you know all the words.

In a group, divide up the list of things to bring so that you have about three items each. (Alternatively, if you cannot work in a group, choose three of the items on the list.)

Brainstorm some questions to ask about the items on your list (you can do this in English). List them in the appropriate boxes on the worksheet.

Add any additional general questions you may have about going to Indonesia (eg What else should I take? How long is the journey?).

Share your questions with your group. Add any questions from other people you think are important.
