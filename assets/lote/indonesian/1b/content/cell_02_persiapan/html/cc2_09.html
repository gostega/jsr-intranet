Complete the following tasks to evaluate and consolidate what you have learned in this section.

Bicaralah!

You will respond to a number of questions based on information in the map of Indonesia.

Map it!

On your personalised interactive map, add a marker to your closest International Airport. In Indonesian, summarise a few key things you have learnt in this unit. The following phrases may be helpful:

Budi bilang bahwa … Dia menyadarkan saya bahwa …
Budi said that … S/he made me aware of …
Ibu Indra memberitahukan kami bahwa … Saya harus ingat …
Ibu Indra informed us that … I have to remember …

Tulislah!

Extend the email to Rolf (the German participant in Tantangan Indonesia) you started earlier, to give as much advice as you can about travelling in Indonesia. You should pay attention to:

  • conventions of emails
  • register and tone of language (eg personal pronouns, giving orders versus making recommendations)
  • cultural sensitivities relating to place, clothing and religion.
