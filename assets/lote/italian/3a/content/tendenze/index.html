Tendenze

Tendenze is about trends and fashion. Italians are well known for their passion and fascination with fashion and style and display a desire to belong to and to conform with local neighbourhood trends.

In this topic you will use a variety of authentic texts to identify and analyse information about trends in Italy. This exposure will extend your skills in reading and viewing, and develop your knowledge of Italian colloquial language and expressions.

You will engage in discussions in which you express your point of view on trends in your environment, and you will be given strategies to help you write an extended response using a range of appropriate linguistic resources.

By engaging in these learning activities you will gain an insight into current Italian trends and be challenged to reflect on your situation. Suggested time for working through this section: 16 hours.
