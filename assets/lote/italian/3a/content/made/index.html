Made in Italy

The focus of this topic is Made in Italy. It is explored through examples that illustrate the significance of il marchio for Italy's image, international recognition and economy.

By engaging with a variety of texts, you will gain an insight into this essential component of Italy's makeup and the cultural representations that the texts portray. You will address this aspect in your own environment and reflect on the meaning of Made in Australia.

The opportunities to make connections with the Italian youth community will be investigated.

Suggested time for working through this topic is 15 hours.
