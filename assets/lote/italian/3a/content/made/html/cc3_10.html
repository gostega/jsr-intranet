Iniziativa italiana

A local Italian association would like to promote the Italian connection for the young generation with an interest in Italian.

The president of the association is asking for proposals for nuove iniziative that will help to make this connection for i giovani.


Your Italian teacher would like to involve the class in this venture as it will enhance the Italian learning experience. Students are asked to plan un'attività to submit to the president of the Italian association.

The class is given the following task.

Un'iniziativa italiana

  1. Plan un'attività/evento with an Italian component aimed at the following market:
    • students studying Italian
    • giovani of Italian background.
  2. Develop promotional material for the event.

GUIDA: Un' iniziativa italiana

Brainstorm

In your group, brainstorm activities that will be of interest to the target group.

The activity may be

  • cultural/linguistic in nature
  • celebrating a particular event
  • simply having fun in un ambiente italiano.

It does not have to be a big event which requires a lot of organisation, time and resources. You can keep it simple but still achieve the aim of the event.

Selection

As a result of the brainstorming session, you may have several suggestions.

You need to select one activity. Aim for group consensus. Each member explains the reason why a particular event will appeal to the target market.

Planning

Plan the event.
