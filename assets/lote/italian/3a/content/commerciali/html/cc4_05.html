Listening strategy

Making meaning from texts


  1. What is the purpose of the exhibition?

    To exhibit the talent and art of forty Australian designers.
  2. Provide the following details of the exhibition:
    Triennale di Milano
    10 May to 29 June
  3. List five areas of design that will be exhibited.

    furnishing, lighting, textiles, glassware, ceramics, metal items, jewellery accessories
  4. Why is the Triennale di Milano a perfect location for this exhibition?

    It's the centre of design recognised all over the world.
  5. How will Australian designers benefit from this exhibition?
    Milan being the international capital of design, it offers a unique opportunity to expose/exhibit Australian design at a global level.
