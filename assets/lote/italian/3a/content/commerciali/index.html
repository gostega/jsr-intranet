Rapporti commerciali

Rapporti commerciali looks at the trade links that exist between Italy and Australia. The introduction of the euro and its effect on European communities will be addressed with an emphasis on the Italian experience.

By exploring a variety of texts you will gain a basic understanding of these two aspects.

You will engage in conversation on a variety of topics, listen to radio excerpts and explore relevant online material. Strategies will be provided to help develop your skills and confidence in speaking.

Suggested time for working through this topic is 10 hours.
