Conversation

Useful tips to help you with conversation:

  • identify appropriate information to prepare prior to a conversation
  • respond to the questions and non-verbal cues of other speaker(s), providing relevant information associated with the task
  • extend and develop your response to demonstrate the extent of your knowledge of Italian
  • initiate and maintain conversation where appropriate
  • ask for clarification if you don't understand the question
  • provide examples of your intercultural understanding
  • interact with other speaker(s), using appropriate body language and register
  • speak naturally and show enthusiasm.
