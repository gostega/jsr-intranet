Writing strategies

  • When you read in Italian, make notes of any new phrases and vocabulary that may be useful in your own writing.
  • When you read or view different text-types, you will notice different features of the language. These will assist in the production of your own texts.
  • Revise language points regularly and refer to previous activities.
Congiunzioni

Conjunctions are words that link several ideas into one sentence.

Try and use a variety of conjunctions in your writing to make it more sophisticated.

Vocabulary/phrases

When you engage with a text make a note of:

  • interesting phrases
  • new vocabulary and facts
  • anything you can incorporate into your writing.

Incorporate a variety of interesting, creative and authentic language.

Never translate word for word from English. Get into the habit of thinking in Italian.


Linguistic elements

  • Review linguistic elements on an ongoing basis. This will enable you to write increasingly complex sentence structures with more accuracy.
  • Review corrected work. Read over earlier pieces to check that you are not making the same mistakes.

Text types

When you engage with texts, note the features of the different text types, ie an email and a blog.

Apply what you learn to your own writing.

Use features that are appropriate to the following contexts:

  • content
  • purpose
  • audience.

Practise regularly

  • Look for opportunities (including authentic contexts) to write in Italian.
