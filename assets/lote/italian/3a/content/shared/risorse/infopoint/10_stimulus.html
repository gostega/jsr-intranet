Questions to initiate the conversation

Questions for further discussion
The stimulus is used as a prompt for further discussion.
Think about the following:
Which topic does the stimulus relate to?
How does it relate to the unit that you are studying?
Think about what possible questions may be asked in relation to the focus and content of unit.
