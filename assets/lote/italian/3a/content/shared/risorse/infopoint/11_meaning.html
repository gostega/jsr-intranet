Making meaning from texts

Useful tips that help you to make meaning from texts:

  • use English as a basis, ie look for cognates
  • skim/scan the text to locate key words
  • guess the meaning from key words
  • highlight/summarise key points
  • use visual cues and headings to predict meaning
  • use clues such as body language and intonation (spoken texts)
  • use knowledge of linguistic elements to infer meaning
  • refer to a linguistic resource
  • use a bilingual and/or monolingual dictionary to check meanings of unfamiliar vocabulary
  • collaborate with other students.
