Nomi composti

Compound nouns are made up of two or more words joined together. The plural form of compound nouns generally follows the normal rule for plural nouns, but may vary according to the noun.

For example:

Singolare Singular Plurale Plural
la ferrovia railway le ferrovie railways
l’autostrada highway/freeway le autostrade highways/freeways
l’arcobaleno rainbow gli arcobaleni rainbows
la banconota banknote le banconote banknotes
il cavolfiore cauliflower i cavolfiori cauliflowers
l’altoparlante loudspeaker gli altoparlanti loudspeakers
il passaporto passport i passaporti passports


There are some instances in which the first word also is changed to the plural form. For example: la cassaforte – le casseforti.
