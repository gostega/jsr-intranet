Il cinema italiano

Al cinema insieme!

Uno dei tanti vantaggi per chi conosce una lingua straniera è che permette di accedere ed entrare nel mondo della società a cui appartiene la lingua. È un vero privilegio condividere esperienze orientate ad un pubblico autentico, le quali danno la possibilità di capire meglio la cultura.



In this attività you will have the opportunity to do just that! You will view an Italian film and be exposed to authentic language in an enjoyable context. Furthermore, you will have the privilege of entering into the Italian world and observe how Italians portray themselves. As an outsider you will be able to experience an authentic slice of Italian life!

Buona visione!

La radio e la televisione (including il telegiornale) also reflect a country's culture and provide an authentic up-to-date portrayal. Thanks to modern technology puoi entrare in questo mondo con un clic!

Take advantage of these possibilities. They will expand your insight into Italian society and increase your confidence in understanding native Italian speakers in authentic contexts.

Visione di un film italiano

Select a recent Italian film.

  • It's important that the film is recent so that the culture portrayed and language used is a realistic reflection of present-day Italy.
  • Make sure that the film is actually Italian and not a dubbed foreign film.

Alcuni suggerimenti:

  • Notte prima degli esami
  • Caterina va in città
  • Manuale d'amore 1

Before viewing the film read qualche recensione so that you have an understanding of what the film is about. Reviews can be easily accessed on Italian Internet sites. For example http://it.movies.yahoo.com.
