Introduction

Welcome to Italian Unit 3A – Made in Italy.

In Made in Italy you will explore the current trends in Italy, cultural and teenage identity as portrayed in the media and the significance of il marchio Made in Italy for Italy’s image, international recognition and economy.

You will examine the presence of Italy and Italian speaking communities in your own environment and have the opportunity to reflect on your personal connection to all things Italian.

Modern technology will allow you to have Italy alla portata da mano and have immediate access to the heartbeat of Italian life.

This resource contains four topics. These topics are accessible by clicking on the menu on the left-hand side.

How to navigate this resource

Each topic has four separate sections which can be accessed by clicking the tabs at the top of the screen.

These sections are:

  • Risorse
  • Attività
  • Tasks
  • Print content

Risorse lists the linguistic element and information relevant to the topic.

Attività contains a number of outcome focus activities which can be accessed via the drop down menu when the mouse is on the button. It also contains a range of activities that will allow you to put the skills and knowledge into practice.

The Tasks section contains assessment tasks covering all aspects of the topic.

The Print content tab gives you the option to print out the entire topic.

When you are ready, select a topic to begin.
