Attività 2 Facciamo festa!

Special occasions are an important part of family life. Some people put a lot of effort into organising family celebrations.

  1. The following language is related to celebrations. Match the correct Italian word with its English translation.

Onomastico refers to a person’s name day. This is the feast day of the saint they have been named after. Most Italians have at least one name that is of religious significance and this is celebrated on their name day. In the past, name days used to be celebrated more than actual birthdays.

Italian e-cartoline sites will often have the category onomastico for the selection of cards.

To view some examples, vai al sito web www.cartolineparlanti.net, seleziona Onomastico.

One of the events that most companies cater for is 18 anni.

In Italy turning 18 is highly significant and this birthday tends to be celebrated alla grande!

The 21st birthday, however, is not considered to be a special birthday the way it is in Australia.
