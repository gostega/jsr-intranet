Made in Italy!

The project manager from the ICCI made reference to il prodotto Made in Italy in the interview.

Information about Made in Italy products can be found on its website www.madeinitaly.tv.

Madeinitaly.tv is online television dedicated to the Italian genius and the assets which have made Italy world-famous. It’s an excellent site where you can watch videos on a variety of areas such as fashion, typical products, industry and tourism.

Begin with the introduction video. Then select areas of interest to you.

Select italiano for the language option. This will give you language exposure and help you to develop your viewing skills.
