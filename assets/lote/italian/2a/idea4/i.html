I miei

I miei covers family and relationships. Young Italians will talk about their families and you will be given the opportunity to talk about your own family.

Family relationships focus on il rapporto genitori – figli adolescenti. You will be able to share with parents issues concerning teenagers, offer advice and write about your own personal situation and experiences.

You will research La tipologia della famiglia in Italy and in Australia and this will enable you to compare how families are made up in both countries.

There are many Italians here who form part of the Australian ‘family’. Their presence in this country along with their influence on trade and business will be explored.

The suggested time for working through this topic is twelve sessions.
