Attività 4 Il rapporto genitori – figli adolescenti

Role play – Pair work

Student A Student B
  • Your father is very strict.
  • You find it easy to speak with your mother.
  • You are not allowed to go wherever you want.
  • Your parents drive you and pick you up when you go out.
  • Your mother is very protective.
  • Your father listens to your problems.
  • You can go out with your friends on the weekend but not during the week.
  • Your parents always want to know who you are going out with and where you are going.

 

Student A Student B
  • Your mother is like a friend to you.
  • You can talk about everything to your parents.
  • Your parents are strict about study. They will not allow you to go out unless you finish your homework. You usually go out on the weekend.
  • Your parents prefer to drive you when you go out but are flexible. It depends on the situation.
  • You get on really well with your father.
  • You don’t get on with your mother. You clash a lot because you have similar personalities.
  • You find it difficult to talk to your parents about your problems. You prefer to speak to your friends about them.
  • Sometimes your parents are strict, sometimes they are really liberal. You can’t work them out!
