Amici.net

Amici.net is about online communication. It will give you the opportunity to make contact and interact with Italian teenagers online.

This is a fantastic opportunity for you to use your knowledge of Italian in a real context and to learn about Italian youth culture. Having amici virtuali in Italia can be the beginning of a great experience and will make your study of Italian very stimulating.

The Internet also allows you to access an incredible number of Italian websites on a vast array of topics. It's an exciting and invaluable tool for language learning.

The suggested time for working through this topic is ten sessions.
