Rapporti

Benvenuti all’italiano!

Rapporti is about relationships. They relate to friends, family and school. These aspects are explored from the student’s perspective and that of their Italian teenage counterparts. It will broaden your understanding of what it’s like being a teenager in Italy and provide the opportunity to compare the similarities and differences between the two cultures.

The impact of technology and globalisation related to Rapporti will also be addressed.

This unit consists of five topics listed on the left hand side.

Each topic contains four sections:

Introduce (I) – provides an overview of the content cell.

Discover (D) – provides the resources to help you work through the attività in the Engage section.

Engage (E) – provides you with a range of tasks to practise the language in a variety of contexts.

Apply (A) – gives you an opportunity to apply your skills and knowledge.

Click on I, D, E & A on the top right hand corner to navigate between these four sections.

Select a topic on the left to begin.
