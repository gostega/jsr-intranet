Viewing strategies

  • Relax! Don't stress. Being relaxed and positive helps you to be more receptive.
  • Look for contextual clues that help you guess the meaning of what is going on.
  • Focus on the questions or the response that you are expected to provide.
  • Look at body language – this may also help you make meaning of the content.
  • Watch the video several times to familiarise yourself with the voices and the content.
  • Make notes of words/phrases that you are able to understand.
  • Replay films/recordings – look and listen for different things each time.
  • Enjoy the viewing!
Viewing authentic material

If you don't understand everything or not very much non ti preoccupare! You are not expected to.

Remember that you are listening to native speakers so the speed may be fast. The reality is that it is normal speed for a native speaker! It is probably the same speed that you use speaking in English.
