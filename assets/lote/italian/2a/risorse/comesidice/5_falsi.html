Some modern English words have found their way into the colloquial Italian language, creating a new language known as 'Italiese', a combination of standard italiano and inglese. For example, you'll hear sono single - 'I'm single'. Italiese is often found in sporting terms: for example lo sprint e il dribbling. Other examples are: la babysitter and lo stress. Sometimes these italiese words are confusing - il mister is a manager, il kit is DIY (Do It Yourself - handyman work), and il night is a nightclub!
