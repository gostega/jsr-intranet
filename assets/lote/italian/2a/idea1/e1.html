Here are two other ways to start the expressions above.

(io) vorrei I would like
mi piacerebbe + infinitive verb + goal I would like to

The bolded words in the above resolutions are all verbs.

In English a verb in the infinitive can be identified by putting the word ‘to’ in front of it, for example to eat, to fly, to feel.

A verb in Italian is recognised by its ending. Italian verbs in the infinitive end in –are, –ere, –ire.

If you need help, refer to Elementi Linguistici – I verbi – Il presente.
