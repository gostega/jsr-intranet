Una guida: Buoni propositi per l’anno nuovo

Consider the following points.

  • What would you like to be involved in?
  • What would you like to achieve?
  • What friendships and relationships would you like to establish?
  • Include some goals that are specific to your study of Italian.
    For example: Voglio praticare l’italiano il più possibile.
  • Refer to the information covered in this section for espressioni e parole utili.
  • Be creative! Use the dictionary to help you.
