Buoni propositi

Attività 3 Propositi e sogni per l’anno nuovo

With the start of the New Year the local Italian community radio is giving teenagers the opportunity to share their nuovi propositi e sogni with other listeners.

Listen to i ragazzi talking about their goals and dreams for the coming year. Then match the person with the resolutions each makes. Select from the list provided.


  • Start by looking up some of the key words in your dictionary.
  • Replay the audio as often as required to complete the activity.
