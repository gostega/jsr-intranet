Buoni propositi

Buoni propositi is all about setting goals per l'anno nuovo. Italians have un modo di dire: Anno nuovo, vita nuova! The new year is an ideal time for making resolutions and for setting personal goals to help you fulfil your dreams and aspirations.

You will have the opportunity to exchange information with other teenagers and set your own goals with a focus on the new school year.

Auguroni per l'anno nuovo e per lo studio dell'italiano.

The suggested time for working through this topic is three to four sessions.

Anno nuovo, vita nuova!
