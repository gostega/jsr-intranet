One of the features of teenage e-zines is a forum where young people can share their views and opinions. The following forum site is very light-hearted and asks the question ‘What would you take with you to a desert island?’

Seven teenagers had placed importance on thirteen different things. Can you use the highlighter to identify all the objects mentioned by each person?


*saranno – they will be

*sarà – it will be

These are the forms of the future tense of the verb essere. You will be learning about the future tense later on in the course.

Did you notice the use of un, una, un’, uno in the responses? These words are used before nouns and mean ‘a/an/one’. They are called indefinite articles.

un mese su un’isola deserta

un televisore, un telecomando
