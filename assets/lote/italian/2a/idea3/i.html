Noi giovani

Noi giovani is about the world of teenagers. It looks at various aspects of the teenage scene like l’amicizia, l’importanza del gruppo and le tendenze. You will be able to share in other teenagers’ opinions and express your own on teenage forums and talk back shows.

You will be encouraged to access online Italian teenage magazines and radio.

Aspects specific to Italian teenagers will also be looked at thus enabling you to compare their world to your own reality.

The suggested time for working through this topic is 10–12 sessions.
