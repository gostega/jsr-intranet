Noi giovani

In the magazine article in Attività 3, readers are asked to send in an article on the topic of teenage spending and fashion in their area.

Write an article for the magazine on Le tendenze da te.

To help you find out the information, prepare a survey to ask other students.

Focus on the following areas:

  • oggetto/bene più importante
  • spese/acquisti
  • abbigliamento e look
  • tendenze e colore
    •

Here are five sample questions to help you get started.

  • Qual è il bene più importante per te?
  • Cosa ti piace comprare?
  • Il look è importante per te?
  • Quale colore è ‘in’ questa stagione?
  • Quale indumento/articolo d’abbigliamento va di moda questa stagione?
    •

When developing your survey questions, try and avoid having questions where the answer is just sì or no. Try to get as much qualitative information as possible.

If you need to review how to ask questions, refer to Elementi linguistici – Interrogativi.

Develop a format that is suited to your sondaggio. Allow room to record the responses.

Survey students in your school and record the answers. Then, write an article to send in to the magazine. Base the content on the information that you collected from the survey. Include relevant visual material.

You may wish to widen your research by looking at teenage magazines aimed at local teenagers.
