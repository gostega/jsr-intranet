Did you notice the following negative phrases in the responses?

Non è vero.

Non siamo passivi.

In Italian, a verb is made negative by simply putting the word non in front of the verb or the auxiliary (helping) verb in the passato prossimo.

Affirmative sentence

È vero. It's true.

Negative sentence

Non è vero. It's not true.


Refer to Elementi linguistici – Espressioni negative to find about other negative expressions.
