Spazio scuola

Spazio scuola is about school life and relationships. You will find out about the Italian school system and will be able to compare it to your own school system. The value of school excursions/trips and modern-day school issues will be addressed. You will explore new technological developments and the challenges and exciting possibilities that they present in the world of education.

The suggested time for working through this topic is 15 to 16 sessions.
