Una gita scolastica migliore!

Students at your school have asked to be involved in the organisation of the next school trip/camp.The student committee of which you are part of has been asked to submit a proposal for a school trip/camp. Together with other members of the committee, develop a proposal to submit to the school leadership team.

To help you with the proposal, look at the following guidelines.

  • Select the aim and type of trip. For example:

    • campeggio/gita per gli studenti di scambio
    • educazione fisica
    • musica
    • teatro
    • lingua e cultura.

  • Focus on the following areas:

    • dove andare
    • attività da fare
    • programma
    • valore didattico.
  • Length of la gita – 2/3 days

In developing the proposal, keep the following in mind:

  • the interests of the students the trip/camp is aimed at
  • the budget
  • the activities that you can realistically offer students
  • keeping within the boundaries of school expectations
  • the group’s personal experience of camps/excursions – brainstorm to help you develop a better one. Avoid activities that didn’t work (attività da evitare) and build on those that were successful (attività consigliate).
