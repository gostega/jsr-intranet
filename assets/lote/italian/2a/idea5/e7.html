Attività 7 Come vivere meglio la gita scolastica

Listen to students give their opinions on how to make the most of school trips/excursions on Radio Giovani.

I giovani used many espressioni impersonali in their responses. For example:

Fabio : Si visita un posto, si ammirano dei monumenti, si ascoltano delle spiegazioni ...
You visit a place, you admire the monuments, you listen to explanations ...

Martina: Si stampano le fotografie, si mettono in ordine le cartoline ...
You print the photos, you put in order/organise the postcards ...

The impersonal construction is used where the subject is not specified. In English, the equivalent translation is ‘one’, ‘you’ or ‘people’.

Refer to Elementi linguistici – Espressioni impersonali to review the use of impersonal expressions.
