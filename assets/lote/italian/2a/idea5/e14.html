Attività 14 Nuove generazioni attraverso la scuola ed Internet

Con le nuove tecnologie, la scuola deve competere con altre risorse formative. Deve essere attenta ai bisogni e alle strategie cognitive della nuova generazione.

Risorse digitali, lavagne interattive, ambienti virtuali di apprendimento rappresentano oggi la nuova frontiera della didattica.

Listen to the details of this initiative.

In this activity you will look at some of the new ways in which technological challenges and opportunities have been taken on and integrated into the school environment.

Write brief notes in English as to Perché una radio?

Why did the school and students choose the radio as a medium for their project?

To listen to the radio, click on Come ascoltarci on the school’s homepage and select Le trasmissioni.

It will be interesting for you to listen to the various segments of the school’s web radio program.

You may find that it’s difficult for you to understand very much. Don’t worry! There are several reasons for this. Firstly the students are native speakers and therefore will be speaking fast and perhaps not very clearly. Furthermore, being a school radio it’s an amateur production and the quality may not be the best.

Nevertheless, it will give you an idea of the information that students are transmitting on the radio.

If you have problems accessing this site you can also find information about this initiative at http://edublog.altervista.org, go to Recent Posts on the right-hand side and select La prima web radio scolastica in Italia.

La scuola siamo noi è uno spazio web dedicato alla diffusione dei più vivi e innovativi progetti online.

To access some innovative school projects go to www.indire.it and select la scuola siamo noi, then click on to the website.

You can also access La scuola siamo noi website from the RadioFermi homepage at http://radiotuttifermi.altervista.org.

La Fragola.it è un e-giornale delle scuole.

Students can talk about their school and their reality, express their opinions and share them with other students in other Italian schools.

Go to www.lafragola.kataweb.it to find out more about this initiative. On this site you will also find out how to create an online newspaper.
