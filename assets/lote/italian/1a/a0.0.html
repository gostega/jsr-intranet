Voglia d’amicizia

Voglia d’amicizia is all about new friendships. You’ll learn how to introduce yourself, ask questions and have a conversation in Italian. You’ll have the chance to meet new friends online, communicate with Italian teenagers, find out about the loves and hates of teenagers and what is important to them. You will also have the opportunity to explore some aspects of Italian culture!

This part is divided into three sections:

  • Incontri is about introductions and meeting people. Through a variety of incontri, including meeting Italian teenagers online, you’ll learn how to provide personal information about yourself and others.

  • Conosciamoci helps you develop a relationship and find out more about a person. You will learn how to speak about personality and character traits.

  • Amori ma non solo looks at the amori and odi of teenagers in Australia and in Italy. You’ll discover what it’s like to be a teenager in Italy and be able to compare aspects of your own life with those of teenagers in Italy.
Let’s start with Incontri.
