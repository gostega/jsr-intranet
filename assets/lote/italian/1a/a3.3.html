In Italy teenagers can ride a motorino when they are 14 years old. It is common for students to go a scuola or in centro for example in motorino.
Scooters can travel at a speed of 45 kilometres per hour. Some teenagers like to make modifications to the engine so that they can go faster.

You can now go to Extra – Il mio nuovo motorino for an exciting activity on scooters.
