Il presente of -ere verbs

How to conjugate (form) an -ere verb:
Step 1: Drop the -ere at the end of the verb
Step 2: Replace the -ere with a different ending for each subject pronoun (person)

Let’s conjugate the verb:

scrivere
io scrivo I write
tu scrivi you write
lui scrive he writes
lei scrive she writes
noi scriviamo we write
voi scrivete you write (plural)
loro scrivono they write
Formal
Lei scrive you write
Loro scrivono you write (plural)

Did you notice that the endings for io, tu and noi are the same as they are for -are verbs?

Other common -ere verbs:
vivere to live
vedere to see
conoscere to know
descrivere to describe
perdere to lose
ripetere to repeat
ridere to laugh
sorridere to smile
ricevere to receive
