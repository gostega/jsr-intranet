Come stai? (Informal)

This is used when young people are talking to friends, family and other young people.

Come sta? (Formal)

This is used usually when young people are talking to adults, or adults are speaking to other adults in a formal situation.

To answer the question about how you feel you can say:

(Sto) bene grazie       or       Bene grazie

It is not necessary to use sto. It can be left out.

Refer to Come stai? for other ways of expressing how you feel.
