Aggettivi - Il singolare

Aggettivi are describing words. In Italian an adjective usually follows the noun (thing or person) it is describing. For example, Sono carina.

In English the adjective is usually placed before the noun. For example:

Il ragazzo è simpatico - The boy is nice.
La ragazza è simpatica - The girl is nice.

In Italian aggettivi change to agree with what is being spoken about.
There are two groups of aggettivi in Italian:

1. Adjectives that end in o in their original form#.
