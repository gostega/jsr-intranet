Domande

To ask a question that can be answered with sí or no is very easy in Italian.
A statement can be turned into a question by putting a question mark at the end of the sentence instead of a full stop. Per esempio:

(Lui) è italiano He is Italian.
(Lui) è italiano? Is he Italian?
(Loro) hanno sedici anni They are sixteen years old.
(Loro) hanno sedici anni? Are they sixteen years old?
(Tu) parli inglese bene. You speak English well.
(Tu) parli inglese bene? Do you speak English well?

You raise the pitch of your voice towards the end of the sentence so that it sounds like a question.

Interrogativi

Let's look at how to ask questions where you need to provide information in your answer, ie not just sí or no.

Chi? Who? Chi è quella ragazza? Who is that girl?
Come? How?
