Il presente of -ire verbs

How to conjugate (form) an -ire verb:
Step 1: Drop the -ire at the end of the verb
Step 2: Replace the -ire with a different ending for each subject pronoun (person)

Let’s conjugate the verb:

dormire
io dormo I sleep
tu dormi you sleep
lui dorme he sleeps
lei dorme she sleeps
noi dormiamo we sleep
voi dormite you sleep (plural)
loro dormono they sleep
Formal
Lei dorme you sleep
Loro dormono you sleep (plural)


The endings for -ire verbs are almost identical to the -ere verbs.
The only one that is different is the voi form.

Other common -ire verbs:
sentiire to feel/hear
partire to leave
aprire to open
scoprire to discover
