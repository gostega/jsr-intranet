Come ti chiami?

In Italian there is a formal and informal way of asking someone their name.

This is informal. The informal address is used with family, friends and young people.

Come si chiama?

This is formal. The formal address is used when a young person speaks to an adult.

It is also used with adults in formal situations; for example, business people meeting for the first time and in formal situations, eg in shops and in banks.

The answer to the question (whether formal or informal) is:

Mi chiamo ...

To ask the other person their name, there is no need to repeat the whole question.
You can simply say:

E tu? (informal)
E Lei? (formal)
