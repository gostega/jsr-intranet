Attività 10 Il mio mondo

In previous activities, you have seen a variety of presentations by teenagers speaking about il loro mondo.
Now it is your turn!

What can you say about:

  • la tua casa
  • la città/il sobborgo dove abiti
  • il tuo spazio personale?
    •
Prepare a presentation about il tuo mondo.

Think of the audience that you would like to target and use an appropriate delivery mode. You may wish to produce a PowerPoint® presentation, a web page, a mindmap etc … it is up to you!
Include visual material where possible.
Be creative!
This attività requires you to use the language that you have covered in this module.

Tips for writing in Italian
When you read:
  • note down any interesting phrases and new vocabulary that you could use in your own writing
  • write in italiano as much as possible – correspond with an Italian e-pal
  • make good use of the material presented in the activities – this material provides you with a model and the necessary structures
  • check language points that you are not sure of in Elementi linguistici
  • check spellings of words and phrases in Come si dice and in your dizionario.

Una guida

Parte 1
Here are some guidelines to help you with the content. Write answers in italiano to the following questions.

  1. Com’è la tua casa?
  2. Quante stanze ci sono?
  3. Hai la tua camera (da letto)?
  4. C’è un giardino/una piscina?
  5. Descrivi il tuo sobborgo/la tua città.
  6. Cosa c’è da fare nel tuo sobborgo/la tua città?
  7. Cosa c’è da vedere nel tuo sobborgo/la tua città?
  8. Ti piace il tuo sobborgo/la tua città? Perché?

Parte 2
Go through the activities to find relevant expressions and linguistic structures.

Parte 3
Extend your answers as much as possible while maintaining a focus on linguistic accuracy.

Una sfida
Include any other information you consider relevant that you are able to express in italiano.
