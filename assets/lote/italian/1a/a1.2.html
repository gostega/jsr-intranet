Chi sei? 

Another way of finding out who someone is, is to ask Chi sei? 
Evana asked Richard E tu, chi sei? 
Richard replies Io sono Richard. 
It is also correct to answer Evana’s question by saying Mi chiamo Richard.

Essere

Sei and sono come from the verb essere which means 'to be'. Refer to Irregular verb - Essere to find out how the verb works.
