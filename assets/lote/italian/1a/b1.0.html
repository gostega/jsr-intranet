Dove abito

Dove abito introduces you to the concept of housing in Italy. It will be interesting to compare this with your own reality. You will be able to surf the Internet to gain a better understanding of this aspect of Italian life.

Work through the activities. If you would like to find out more or to extend your exposure to Italian, go to Extra for some attività interessanti.

In Dove abito you will learn how to:
  • recognise various rooms in the house
  • describe your house
  • compare Italian and Australian housing
  • use the indefinite article
  • use simple prepositions
  • use possessives (singular form).
You will revise also the plural of nouns.

The suggested time for working through this section is six sessions.
