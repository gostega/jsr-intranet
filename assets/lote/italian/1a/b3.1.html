Attività 1 Lucca – La mia città

In the Dove abito section we looked at Maurizio’s apartment. Now let’s look at la città where he lives.

Lucca è molto fortunata! It is an ideal city: small, tranquil and not spoilt by modern buildings or tourism.

Maurizio directed you to the turismo website to find out more about Lucca. If you have problems with this sito, try a general search on www.yahoo.it. Type Lucca in the cerca box and view this delightful, typical Tuscan town.

An Italian’s idea of la campagna is usually within short driving distance from a major centre. The distance from one country town to another is also very small. It is not very common to see large open spaces and isolated country towns in Italy.
Maurizio uses many aggettivi to describe Lucca, for example:
I negozi sono carini. - The shops are cute.
le discoteche grandi - the big discos
The aggettivi in these examples are in the plural form. In Italian aggettivi change to agree with what is being spoken about.
Refer to Aggettivi – Il plurale to find out about the plural form of adjectives.
In the description of Lucca, Maurizio uses the following expressions:
nelle città - in the cities delle ville - some villas nel centro - in the centre degli alberi - some trees
Nelle, delle, nel, degli are called articulated prepositions.
Refer to Preposizioni articolate to find out how these are formed.
