The objective of the following five exercises is to give you practice using the definite article.

3. Fill in the missing articolo determinative for masculine and feminine nouns.

* For some words it is necessary to add an h when forming the plural. This is to maintain the same sound as in the singular form, eg
le musiche
le amiche
le discoteche.
You will learn more about this as you progress through the course.
