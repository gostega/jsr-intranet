Il mio mondo il tuo mondo

Il mio mondo il tuo mondo focuses on the personal world of the home and where you live. You will engage in activities associated with these aspects of your world. You will also explore the world of your Italian teenage counterparts. It will help broaden your understanding of what housing and living are like in Italy and provide you with the opportunity to explore the similarities and differences of these two worlds.

Technology plays an essential role. The Internet allows you to access the Italian world and observe the Italian reality.

This part has been divided into three sections:

  • In Dove abito you will learn to talk about your house. It also introduces you to the Italian concept of housing.

  • Il mio spazio personale explores the private space of la camera and the importance it has in the teenage reality!

  • In Cosa c'è da fare e vedere? you will learn to give and exchange information about the area you live in. You will be given an insight into some Italian cities from the perspective of teenagers.
