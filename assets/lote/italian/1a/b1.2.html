Ripasso

These teenagers are talking about where they live. Click on an individual to listen and read the details, then click on the floor plan that represents their house/apartment. When you have finished, click on the risposte button to check how you go.

In Italian when speaking about where you live, you use:
a before a city
in before a country.
Per esempio:
Abito a Milano.
Abito a Perth.
Abito in Italia.
Abito in Australia. 		I live in Milan.
I live in Perth.
I live in Italy.
I live in Australia.
This is different from English. In is used for both a city and a country.
Le preposizioni semplice has a detailed explanation on simple prepositions.
