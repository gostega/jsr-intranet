Amori ma non solo!

In Amori ma non solo! you will learn to speak about your passions, likes and dislikes. You will also have the opportunity to gain an insight into il mondo dei giovani. You will discover what they like and what is important to them.

Work through the activities. If you would like to find out more about Italian teenagers or extend your exposure to Italian, go to Extra. There are many interesting ways for you to experiment with la lingua italiana! You can enter a forum or even search for your dream scooter.
Vale la pena!

In this section you will:
  • discover what Italian teenagers like to do
  • learn to use the relevant forms of piacere to talk about your likes/dislikes and those of other people
  • learn about il genere dei sostantivi
  • learn about l’articolo determinativo
  • revisit aggettivi.

The suggested time for working through this section is twelve sessions.
