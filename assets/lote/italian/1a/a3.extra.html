Personaggi

Go to www.bambini.rai.it. Choose the activity on i personaggi.
This activity gives you the opportunity to experience what Italian children are exposed to on the Internet. It asks you to write the questions that you would like to ask your favourite personality about their likes and dislikes. These will be used to interview the character.
You may not be familiar with the characters … ma non importa! Ti puoi divertire lo stesso!
