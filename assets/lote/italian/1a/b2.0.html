Il mio spazio personale

La camera (da letto) is an important aspect of a teenager’s life. Usually it represents a place to go for privacy, to relax, to be yourself. In Il mio spazio personale you will find out how other teenagers feel about their personal space. You will also have the opportunity to design una camera di sogno.

In Il mio spazio personale you will:

  • learn language related to la camera da letto
  • explore the significance of la camera for teenagers
  • learn about regular –ere verbs in the present tense
  • learn about regular –ire verbs in the present tense.

The suggested time for working through this section is seven sessions.
