An interesting way of finding out what the housing is like in Italia is to look at real estate. This will give you a good insight as the information is authentic and immediate.

Agenzia immobiliare

Cercasi casa

Let’s do an Internet search. Go to sito http://www.systemacasa.it/ to search for an apartment or house.
When you enter the sito, you will see the search screen to la ricerca di casa tua in the centre.
Fill in the details in order to refine your search. For example:

Categoria dell’immobile Residenziale
Contratto Vendita (for sale)
Provincia Roma
Prezzo massimo 400,000
Numero locali 3

If you have problems accessing this site, try the following search engine:
Sito: www.yahoo.it
Cerca: appartamenti/case in vendita
Scegli la città where you want to search for an apartment/house, eg Roma, Milano, Bologna.
Then complete the writing exercises in worksheet_B1.Extra.
