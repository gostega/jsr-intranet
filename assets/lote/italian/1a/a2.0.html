Conosciamoci

In Conosciamoci you will learn how to describe yourself and others. You will also be able to find out about yourself and your friends from a personality quiz!

This topic builds on the language that was introduced in Incontri.

Work through the activities. If you would like to find out more or to extend yourself, go to Extra where you’ll have a chance to go online and send una cartolina to someone molto speciale, and communicate with a real Italian e-pal.

In Conosciamoci you will learn how to:
  • use the verb essere in the present tense
  • recognise and use aggettivi in the singular form
  • give information about yourself and others.

The suggested time for this section is ten sessions.

Buon proseguimento!
