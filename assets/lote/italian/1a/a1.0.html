Incontri

Incontri is about meeting people and learning how to introduce yourself and others. You’ll meet some teenager italiani and be able to have incontri nuovi in Italia thousands of kilometres away. And you’ll have a glimpse of some of le città where these giovani live.

You will even incontrare two Australian personaggi famosi who have been lured by il fascino d’Italia and now there.

Work through the activities. If you would like to find out more or to extend your exposure to Italian, go to Extra for some attività interessanti. Many Elementi linguistici are introduced in Incontri. Don’t worry if you can’t understand them in great depth at this stage. You just need to know how to use them in the context of introductions and meeting new people. These elementi linguistici will be revisited again throughout the course in a variety of contexts.

In Incontri you will learn how to:
  • greet people
  • use formal and informal language
  • introduce yourself/others
  • use the verb avere with age
  • use the verb essere with introductions
  • speak about how you feel/ask others how they feel
  • use regular –are verbs in the present tense
  • speak about nationality.

The suggested time for working through this section is ten sessions.
