Attività 5 L’ amico/a del cuore

What qualities would you like to find in a friend?
Never really thought about it? Here’s your chance to be creative!
Imagine that you are able to create an amico/a del cuore.
As you breathe life into the person, think about the characteristics that are important to you.
Remember that you’re not creating a robot but a human being with human characteristics, with both virtù e difetti.

Go back to Come sei? to check your understanding of adjectives if you need to.
