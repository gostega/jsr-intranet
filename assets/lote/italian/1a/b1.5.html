Have you ever thought about going on un viaggio studio to Italy?
These opportunities do exist!

There are many Italian language courses for foreign students around Italy. If you plan to go to university, some Australian and Italian università have an arrangement which allows you to undertake a unit of your course in Italia. Borse da studio are also available.

When you study in Italy, the first thing you need is accommodation. Here are three options available from a university website.
