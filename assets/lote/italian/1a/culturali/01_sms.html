Reportage - I giovani e gli SMS

In Italia circolano più di 36 milioni di cellulari e 89 ragazzi su cento (tra i 15 e i 24 anni) ha almeno un telefonino. Di questi l’83 per cento utilizza abitualmente l’SMS (in inglese Short Message Service) cioè, il sistema dei brevi messaggi.

This article is saying that 89 per cent of young people between the ages of 5–24 in Italia have at least one mobile phone. Of these, 83% use SMS regularly as a form of communication.

Some Facts
In Italia: 8 billion SMS messages, an average of 49 million, are sent a day.
Gli SMS is a craze that has hit Italy and the world! For young people it has replaced the phone as a way of interacting with friends and family.
I ragazzi all love to send SMS messages!
The SMS craze is creating problems in schools where gli studenti become distracted as they are busy sending and reading SMS messages instead of focusing on their work in class. Lessons are often interrupted by the ringing of mobiles.
The vast array of personalised ring tones is very creative!

Gli SMS: Il nuovo linguaggio
The result is a new way of communicating using chat line style language and ‘emoticons’ (dall’ inglese emotion and icon).
Here are some of the most frequently used emotions in Italia.
:-* bacio :-D felice
[ ] baci e abbracci :-v parlare
