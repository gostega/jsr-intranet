Attività 9 Una telefonata

Imagine you have met a really cool e-pal! You are so excited you can't wait to tell your friends!
Cosa aspetti? Telephone your amico/a del cuore and tell them your novità.

This activity gives you the opportunity to practise using all the Italian you have learnt so far. Make sure you revise and prepare thoroughly before making the phone call.

Here are some suggestions to help you prepare for this activity.

Cosa puoi dire?

  • Greetings
  • Ask how your friend is.
  • Describe the person.
  • Answer questions that your friend asks about the person. Per esempio:

    - Come si chiama?
    - Quanti anni ha?
    - Di che tipo è?
    - Cosa avete in comune?

Una telefonata has some information to help you.

Being well prepared will enable you to cope with unseen material and help you to respond with language that is not totally rehearsed. It will make the phone conversation more realistic.
It’s a good idea to record your questions and answers and then listen to yourself. This will help you with pronunciation and fluency.

You can use this worksheet_A2.9 to prepare suitable questions and answers.
Enjoy! Good luck! Next you can practise some more by writing an email.
