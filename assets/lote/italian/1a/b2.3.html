Scrivi comes from the verb scrivere to write.
Rifletti comes from the verb riflettere to reflect.
These are known as –ere verbs.

In Voglia d’amicizia you learned how to form regular verbs that end in –are.

Refer to Il presente of –ere verbs to find out how to form the endings for this group of verbs.
