Ancora verbi!
Sergio says ‘Dormo e studio qui soltanto.’ about his room.
Dormo is from the infinitive dormire.
Dormire belongs to the –ire group of verbs.
It is easy to remember the verb endings for the –ire group because they are almost identical to the –ere verbs.
Refer to Il presente of –ire verbs to find out more.
