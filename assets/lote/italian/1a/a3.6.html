L’articolo determinativo

Did you notice the use of il, la, i, le, gli in the above responses? These words have been used before nouns and mean ‘the’. They are called definite articles. In English the definite article has only one form:'the'. In Italian l’articolo determinativo, has different forms according to gender and number.
