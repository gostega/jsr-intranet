Attività 5 Come stai?

You can now introduce yourself in Italian. But you want to be able to say more! Asking someone Come stai? is always a polite way of starting a conversation.

Listen to Chris and Evana.



Chris asks Evana Come stai? This means ‘How are you?’
Evana replies Molto bene grazie which means ‘Very well, thanks.’

To ask the other person how they feel you do not have to repeat the question. You can simply say: 

E tu?  and you?/what about you? (informal)
E Lei?  and you?/what about you? (formal)

Now listen to Chris and signora Lampronte.


Chris asked la signora Lampronte Come sta? He is using the formal (polite) form of address because he is speaking to an adult.

Refer to Come sta? Come stai? to find out more.
