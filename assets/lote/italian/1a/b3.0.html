Cosa c'è da fare e vedere?

In this section, you will learn how to speak about where you live and also discover what it is like to like in an Italian city. You will be given the opportunity to visit le città di Roma e Lucca – un viaggio, virtual of course!

Work through the activities. If you have time, have a go at the activities in the Extra section. They will help to extend your language use and to develop your confidence in italiano.

In Cosa c'è da fare e vedere? you will:

  • learn about the irregular verbs andare and fare
  • explore the use of prepositions
  • look at what there is to see and do in a city
  • visit the cities of Lucca and Roma
  • learn to speak about the area where you live
  • revise aggettivi

The suggested time for working through this section is ten sessions.
