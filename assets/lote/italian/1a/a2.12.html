Attività 12 Un personaggio famoso

In Incontri you were introduced to two australiani, Megan Gale and Marco Bresciano. Why don’t you find out about a famous person in Italy?

Now that you have a better knowledge of how Italian works, it will be a great way to practise the language structures that you have learnt so far.

Which aspects are you interested in? Il mondo di sport, moda fashion, cinema, or musica?
Go to www.yahoo.it and begin your search. Prova being a writer for a magazine! Che divertimento!
You can find a biografia template in worksheet_A2.12. Use it to record the details of a famous person of your choice.

Write in the third person, ie lui or lei (he or she) for a magazine article.
It’s not necessary to put in the il, la etc in front of the word when saying what occupation people have, for example:
           Professione: modella for Megan Gale
           Professione: calciatore for Marco Bresciano.

Una sfida!

What else can you say about the person? What additional information can you provide?

See if you can write any information about Interessi e passatempi and descrizione fisica. If you can’t, non importa! You will be covering these areas later on in the course.

Professioni has some other typical occupations of famous people.
