If you were a participant in Ragazzi In, how would you answer Federico’s questions?

Federico: Ciao. Come ti chiami?
You:

(Say what your name is.)
Federico: Piacere.
You:
(Say 'Pleased to meet you'.)
Federico: Come stai oggi?
You:
(Say how you are feeling and ask how he is feeling.)
Federico: Molto bene grazie. Ho diciassette anni. E tu? Quanti ani hai?
You:
(Tell him how old you are.)
Federico: Dove abiti?
You:
(Tell him where you live and ask him where he lives.)
Federico: Io abito a Roma.


Federico says: lo abito a Roma. This means ‘I live in Rome’.
This is different from English. In italiano you use:

  a for cities in for countries
  abita a Milano abito in Australia
  abitiamo a Sydney abitano in Italia


Refer to Dove abiti? for different ways of asking where a person lives.
