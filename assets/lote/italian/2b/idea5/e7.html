Attività 7 Voglia di scambio! Da solo o in gruppo?

Immagina!

At your school you have the option of going on a cultural study tour with your Italian class or with a student exchange organisation.

Which option will you choose?

Explain to your Italian teacher the reasons for your choice. La conversazione è in italiano.

Include the advantages that you perceive for the option that you have selected. Clicca qui if you need some ideas to get you started.

Student exchange organisation Cultural study tour with your Italian school group
  • live with an Italian family for an extended length of time
  • go to school and meet teenagers your age
  • learn to be independent and cope in situations on your own
  • total immersion in language will improve your Italian.
  • tour different cities and experience cultural events
  • share experience with classmates
  • feel comfortable and safe being with teacher and school group
  • don’t have to spend too much time at school
  • don’t want to be away from family for too long.

To help you prepare for the conversation, it’s a good idea to write some notes in italiano first.

Exchange programs are becoming a common component of the language classroom.

Informati!

Find out which student exchange programs to Italy you are able to access in your città.

Not everyone may have the wonderful opportunity of being an exchange student; however, it’s possible to host a student. Having an Italian student share your house, family, school and friends can be an exciting and rewarding experience. You can learn so much about Italy and the lifestyle from an authentic source.
