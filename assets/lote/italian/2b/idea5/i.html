Eperienze da raccontare

Esperienze da raccontare is about holiday experiences. You will be able to share in a variety of authentic travel experiences of young people, thus giving a realistic portrayal of vacanze vissute. The increasing popularity of blogs allows you also to read online about the travel experiences of your Italian teenage counterparts. You will have the opportunity to be una protagonista and share le tue esperienze di viaggio con il pubblico online!

Blogga e condividi le tue esperienze!

The suggested time for working through this topic is 8 to 10 sessions.
