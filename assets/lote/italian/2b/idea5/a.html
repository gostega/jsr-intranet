Blog: Il mio viaggio a...

Racconta le tue esperienze di viaggio. Scrivi un blog e condividi online la tua esperienza di un viaggio memorabile.

You may prefer to create a home video of your holiday experience with a commentary in italiano to put on an Italian online youth travel site.

To help you write an online blog account of your holiday experience you may find the following sites useful.

http://kataweb.it/blog, select Crea il tuo blog.

http://cronachediviaggio.blogs.it

http://www.vivilastminute.it, select consigli per bloggare!

To help you with the content it’s a good idea to view some entries found at: http://www.studenti.it, select viaggi.

You may also look at other relevant sites that you come across on the Internet.

This will help you identify parole e frasi utili that you can use or modify in your travel account.


Alternative scenario

Che belle vacanze!

Immagina!

You have met an Italian teenager recently with whom you have become good friends. You have kept in touch since he/she has gone back to Italy.

You have just been on holidays and have done lots of exciting things.

Write an email to your Italian friend in italiano in which you describe your holiday experiences.

You will need to use il passato prossimo and l’imperfetto widely to speak about past experiences.



Refer to Elementi linguistici – il passato prossimo and l’imperfetto if you need to review these verbs.
