Attività 2 Ricordi del mio soggiorno in Italia

Immagina!

Il tuo soggiorno in Italia è arrivato alla fine. Sei rientrato/a in Australia da poco. Ma il viaggio non è finito. Ti rimangono tantissimi ricordi di questa esperienza memorabile. Adesso è bello ordinare i tuoi ricordi incollando tutto in un album, con i commenti che ad ogni pagina rinnovano le sensazioni e le emozioni provate durante il viaggio.

Task

Organise your Italian holiday photos and realia. Write captions about your holiday experiences that will help you to remember those wonderful moments!
Clicca qui for further details about this task.

Una guida: Ricordi del mio soggiorno in Italia

  • Travel plans you made in Voglia di vacanze should be used as a focus for the content.
  • Write about the things that you did and your impressions rather than cultural information about cities. (The cultural information has already been addressed in your travel plans.)
  • Include about 10 photos/items. These should be a combination of places that you visited, favourite bars, pizzerie, shops, people that you met and activities in which you were involved. You can also include a brochure of an event that you attended, a train ticket, a menu, among other things. You can use photos and other realia that you require from the Internet or from other relevant sources.

Presentation

Use an appropriate form to present your album.

You may find the following sites useful to help you present your photos:

http://www.flickr.com

http://www.myphotobook.it


Be creative!

You may have already experienced un viaggio in Italia. In this case, your information will reflect a real experience. If, however, you have not been to Italy, you can still provide a convincing account based on the research that you have done in previous topics by creating your own photo album.
