Attività 8 Giovani italiani in viaggio in Australia

Travel to Australia for young Italians and other foreigners is becoming very possible due to the many casual holiday work opportunities that exist. The Working Holiday Visa allows young people to combine travel with work experience. This situation gives rise to increased demand/opportunities in the backpacker Australian travel market.

To find out more about the Working Holiday Visa, go to http://turismo.stile.it.
Search for Australia largo ai giovani.

Once you have accessed the article, complete the following attività.

If you have difficulty finding the article do a general search on The Australian Working Visa for young people.

Backpacker tour

You are doing a tour guide course. As part of your studies you have been asked to develop in italiano a two-day tour in your state aimed at the Italian backpacker market and using appropriate language for the target group.

Address the following points:

  • Choose a location of interest for foreign backpackers.
  • Select places of interest for the destination that you have chosen.
  • Develop activities that are suitable for the area chosen and also for the target market.
  • Use suitable language to connect with the target audience.

  • Restrictions:

    Duration – two days

    Cost – budget backpacker market.

Refer to Linguaggio dei Tour for frasi e parole utili in the previous section Eperienze da raccontare to help you develop the tour.

You may wish to include web links where visitors can find useful tourist information ie transport, shopping, tourism, holidays.
