Pronomi diretti e pronomi indiretti

In Lorenzo’s information there are examples of direct and indirect object pronouns – pronomi diretti e pronomi indiretti.

Ho imparato molto della cultura italiana studiandola in Italia.

I learnt a lot about the Italian culture by studying it in Italy.

In this sentence la is referring to la cultura italiana.

la is an example of i pronomi diretti.



Direct object pronouns have been covered in previous units.

La famiglia gli regala una coscia di prosciutto.

The family gives him a leg of ham as a present.

In this sentence gli is referring to lo zio.

Gli is an example of i pronomi indiretti.

By using direct and indirect pronouns, you avoid repeating the same word again.

Refer to Elementi linguistici – I pronomi diretti to review the use of direct object pronouns.

I pronomi indiretti to find out about indirect object pronouns

Some universities in Australia have agreements with Italian universities. These allow students to undertake part of their study in an Italian institution and gain credit towards their degree. The agreements are reciprocal and so Italian students have the same opportunities.

It can be a great way to complement your study. Not only do you learn about the subject that you are studying but also you have the added bonus of discovering and being immersed in a new culture.
