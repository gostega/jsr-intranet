Andiamo

Benvenuti all’italiano!

Andiamo (Let’s go!) is about travel. It explores the exciting world of travel and holiday experiences from the perspective of i giovani. You will look at what to consider when planning una vacanza in Italia and the language skills required to cope with situations when in Italy. You will also examine your local area as a destination for young Italian travellers.

The increasing popularity of online blogs allows you to share personal travel experiences with other teenagers including your Italian counterparts.

You will have the opportunity to discover the Italian presence nella tua città and are encouraged to develop links with the local Italian community.

This unit consists of six topics listed on the left hand side.

Each topic contains four sections:

Introduce (I) – provides an overview of the topic.

Discover (D) – provides the resources to help you work through the attività in the Engage section.

Engage (E) – provides you with a range of tasks to practise the language in a variety of contexts.

Apply (A) – gives you an opportunity to apply your skills and knowledge.

Click on I, D, E & A on the top right hand corner to navigate between these four sections.

Select a topic on the left to begin.
