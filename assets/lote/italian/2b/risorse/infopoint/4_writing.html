Writing strategies

  • When you read in Italian, make notes of any new phrases and vocabulary that may be useful in your own writing.
  • When you read or view different text-types, you will notice different features of the language. These will assist in the production of your own texts.
  • Revise language points regularly and refer to previous activities.
Congiunzioni

Conjunctions are words that link several ideas into one sentence.

Try and use a variety of conjunctions in your writing to make it more sophisticated.

Clicca qui for a list of common conjunctions.
