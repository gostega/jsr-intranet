Nomi invariabili

Some nouns in the plural do not change their ending.

Look at the following examples.

il cinema i cinema
la moto le moto
la foto le foto
il caffè i caffè
la città le città
il bar i bar
il film i film
il re i re

Nouns ending in an accent such as –è, –à or –ù are always invariable
(eg la città – le città).

Nouns that have only one syllable do not change in the plural form (eg il re – i re).

Nouns ending in a consonant and that are generally borrowed from another language are invariable (eg il film – i film).
