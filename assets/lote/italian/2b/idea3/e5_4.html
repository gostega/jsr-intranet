Dal parrucchiere

Now go to the hairdresser of your choice and discuss what you want done to your hair.

Consider the following to help you get started:

  • What do you need done to your hair?
  • When can you get your hair done? Do you need an appointment?
  • What is the cost?

Attenzione! Chiedi il prezzo prima di cominciare. Alcuni parrucchieri hanno prezzi salati e forse non sono alla portata delle tasche dei giovani.

Usually there is no need to make an appointment to go to the hairdresser in Italia (in fact, most don’t make appointments) but just turn up and wait your turn. In the big cities the staff tends to be large and individuals specialise in a particular area, ie colouring, blow drying, so it’s easy to get through lots of clients quickly.
