When you are in a foreign country, there are everyday situations that may arise. Not too many tourists prepare themselves for the mundane experiences but tend to focus on the tourist scenarios.

To deal with them, you may find that often the language required is very specific.


Il condizionale, the conditional verb tense is often used in situations where you need to ask for information in a polite way.

For example:

Vorrei prenotare una camera.

Mi potrebbe dire dov’è la fermata della metropolitana?

Refer to Elementi linguistici – Il condizionale for further information on the conditional.
