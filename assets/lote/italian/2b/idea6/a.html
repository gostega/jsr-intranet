L’Italia in Australia

Immagina di essere in Italia durante il periodo natalizio.

Scrivi una e-cartolina di Natale alla famiglia o ad un amico/a.

Write brief details of what you have done in the lead-up to Christmas preparations, and the festive atmosphere that you have noticed. For example, describe i panettoni and i torroni that you have noticed nei supermercati, nelle pasticcerie, e nei bar.

The information should reflect what you have found out from your research about Italian Christmas traditions.

If you wish to select an e-card, go to http://www.cartoline.it.

If you want to send a Christmas card with a real Italian feel, select one from the Natale category.

Don’t forget to include greetings and best wishes for Christmas!

  Buon Natale!
