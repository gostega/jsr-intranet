Guida: Un giorno a ...

  1. Begin by finding out details about cosa c’è da fare e da vedere in the city that you have chosen.

    Listen to the audio guides and look at the photo gallery of the city to help you make your choices for sightseeing.

  2. Select the things that you want to see and do in the city.

    Remember your physical limitations! Plan to see what it is realistically possible to see, do and digest in one day!

    It’s better to see less and take in the experience, rather than see too much and have blurred recollections of what you actually saw!

  3. Choose your point where you will start your day of sightseeing.

    Le ItalyGuides® site also has mappe interattive which can be downloaded. These maps can be very useful in planning cosa vedere e fare in una città.

    Select mappa interattiva della città (if possible download the map).

    Alternatively, you may wish to find another map of your choice.

  4. Include graphics of sights. Number them in the order that you will visit them.

    Visit sights in the order of proximity to each other. You want to use your time efficiently and also you don’t want to wear yourself out!

  5. Write notes in italiano next to the sight as to what you want to see and do at the place.

    This will help you remember cosa c’è da fare e da vedere. In all the excitement and rush you may forget and miss out on seeing some famous marvels!!

  6. Find out how you will you get from one place to another.

    Is walking feasible?

    If public transport is necessary, what is available?

    Is hiring a bike or scooter a possibility?


Make notes in italiano.

Record your writings in a word document.
