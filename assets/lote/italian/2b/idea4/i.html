Vacanze a scelta

Vacanze a scelta opens up the doors to the wonderful world of travel. You will be enticed to explore a vast array of holidays ranging from the classic ever - favourite beach destination to today’s virtual ‘fantasy holiday’.

The focus is on holiday experiences geared at i giovani. You will be able to peruse, at your leisure, a selection of holidays and discover what there is to see and encounter in different destinations.

You will be invited to consider the possibility of combining study/work with holiday travel as a way of better understanding the world and yourself.

The emphasis is in the context of travel in Italy but you will also look at student tourism from the perspective of your own environment and reality.

Rilassati, lasciati andare nel mondo favoloso dei sogni!

The suggested time for working through this topic is 15 sessions.
