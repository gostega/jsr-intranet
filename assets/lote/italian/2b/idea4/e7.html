Attività 7 All’ufficio del turismo


If you are travelling in Italy, the local tourist office is a good place to get information about what to visit and where to stay.

All’ufficio del turismo

  1. The following are some domande tipiche to help you get information at the tourist office about cosa c’è da fare e vedere in un luogo/una città/regione.

    How would you ask these questions in Italian? Write your answers in the space provided. The first one has been done for you.


    Where is the tourist office?

    What can we visit in the area? 
    Che cosa c’è da vedere in questa zona?

    When can we visit the cathedral?  Quando possiamo vedere il duomo?

    When are the museums open?  Quando sono aperti i musei?

    What time does the museum close? 
    A che ora chiude il museo?

    Do you (pl) have any leaflets?  Avete degli opuscoli?

    How much does it cost to get in?   Quanto costa il biglietto di ingresso ?

    Risposte.


    What else would you like to know about the place that you are visiting?

    What other questions do you think would be useful in this context?

    Write them in the section Altro.

    Further questions Altro

    By substituting the noun in the sentence, you can ask questions about a variety of subjects.

    For example:

    Scusi, dov’è l’ufficio del turismo?

    Scusi, dov’è la stazione?

    Scusi, dov’è lo stadio?

    To give you instructions/directions, the person will probably use l’imperativo – the imperative tense.

    For example:

    Scusi, dov’è la stazione? Excuse me, where is the station?
    È in via Mazzini. Vai sempre diritto e poi gira a destra. It’s in via Mazzini. Go straight ahead and then turn right.

    The highlighted verbs are examples of the imperative.

    Go to Elementi linguistici – l’imperativo if you need to review this language point.

    These cities have been selected because they are referred to in some context in Vacanze a scelta. If you prefer, however, you may select a city that is of particular interest to you.

    Limit your search to the city’s ufficio del turismo site.

    To begin your search, go to www.yahoo.it.

    Write the name of la città and ufficio del turismo in the cerca box.

    When you have made your selection, explore the site. This will give you an idea of cosa c’è da fare e vedere.


    Immagina di essere appena arrivato/a in città. Vai all’ufficio del turismo per chiedere informazioni. Quali domande devi/vuoi fare?

    Base your questions on the authentic information on the city’s tourist office website. Ask questions about aspects of the city that interest you.

    Your questions should focus on the following areas:

    • posti d’interesse
    • musei
    • mostre
    • parchi/giardini
    • feste/sagre/eventi.

    Begin by writing your questions.
