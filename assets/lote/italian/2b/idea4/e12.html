Attività 12 Souvenir e regali!

An important aspect of travelling is shopping for those memorable souvenirs for yourself and presents for your family and friends back at home.

What will you buy for yourself to remind you of the wonderful soggiorno that you have experienced nella bella Italia?

What will you buy your family and friends?


Buy tasteful things that are typically Italian. There is no point buying things that can be easily found back at home. This does not mean che devi spendere un patrimonio!
  1. Make a list of the things that you would like to buy for yourself and also in which city you plan to buy them. Write them in the Lista shopping.
  2. Make a list of the gifts you plan to buy for family and friends and also in which city you plan to buy them. Write them in the Lista regali.
  3. Keep within your budget! Use this worksheet to make a note of how much you have allowed for presents and how much you expect to spend on each present.

Shopping online

If you don’t like shopping, take the pain out of the experience and shop online. Also it’s very convenient if you have limited time. You can also avoid the extra stress of carrying extra weight with you and have the goods delivered to your house.
