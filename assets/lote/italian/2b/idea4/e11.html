Attività 11 Cosa farai di bello ...?

Cosa farai per il ponte?

Il ponte is the Italian word for ‘bridge’. It is also used to refer to the custom of taking extra holidays between a public holiday and the following or previous Sunday.

In Italian, working days are called giorni feriali and Sundays and holidays are giorni festivi.
