Vacanze a scelta

  1. All’ufficio del turismo

    Immagina di essere appena arrivato/a in una nuova città. Vai all’ufficio del turismo per chiedere informazioni.

    Quali domande devi/vuoi fare?

    Ask questions about aspects of the city that interest you. For example:

    • posti d’interesse
    • musei
    • mostre
    • parchi/giardini
    • feste/sagre/eventi.

    Task

    Take part in a conversation all’ufficio del turismo between you and l’impiegato/a.

    Ask the necessary questions to find out the information that you require.

    This is not a scripted conversation.

    Remember also to listen to the cues of l’impiegato/a and respond to them in an appropriate manner.

  2. Un invito: Un giorno a ...

    You are in Italy and would like to do a day trip to una città of your choice.

    You have done a lot of research on the city. You have identified and planned what you would like to see and do.

    It will be more interesting if you have un compagno/una compagna di viaggio con cui condividere l’esperienza.

    Task

    Invite a friend or someone you have met during your stay in Italy to explore the city sights with you.

    Send the person an email in which you provide details of when you intend to visit the city and what you plan to do. Send an attachment of your proposed itinerary.

    It’s also a good idea to ask the person for feedback on your itinerary and for other suggestions. Use this worksheet to complete this task.
