Ciao Italia

Attività 1 Alla scoperta del fascino d’Italia!

Making travel plans has never been so easy! Today it’s possible to tailor your travel experience to meet your needs, to find out information about the places where you want to go to and to have bookings made – tutto online!

Before embarking on this Italian experience of a lifetime, you will have the opportunity to find out as much information as possible about the country and places that you are visiting and make essential bookings so that your trip is hassle-free and productive.


Pensieri preliminari

You are thinking about spending some time in Italy.
It’s important to do some research and planning so that you can make well-informed choices.

At this stage this is only brainstorming!

Use the Internet or any other relevant source to find out the information you require.

Write your answers in note form in italiano.



Here are some ideas to get you started:

Una guida: Pensieri preliminari

  • Per quale motivo ti piacerebbe andare in Italia?
  • Quali città/regioni ti interessano? Perché?
  • Quando pensi di andare? È alta/bassa stagione?
  • Per quanto tempo?
  • Con chi viaggi?

Get some flight and accommodation costs. If you are able to be flexible with your departure date, then you may be able to save some money on your flight and accommodation if you don’t travel during l’alta stagione.

The cost for accommodation will also vary, depending on whether you require a single, double room, etc.
