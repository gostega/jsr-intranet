Attività 8 Vai fare la valigia!

Adesso devi soltanto fare la valigia!

Deciding what to take with you on holiday is often a headache! You can pack too much or not enough.

Depending on the type of holiday on which you are going on ie al mare,avventura, ecc you will have different needs. There are things however, that are considered indispensabili!!

Clicca qui for a checklist.

Make sure you know what the items are. Look up in your dictionary any words you don’t understand.

Do you think this is an extremely long list for teenagers? What can you remove?

Select from the list the items you consider necessary and appropriate for your trip.

Bear in mind the check-in luggage regulations and prohibited items.

Do you know what the allowance for hand luggage is? It’s a good idea to find out before you pack.

To find out what le regole per il bagaglio a mano at the airport a Roma, go to the official site at http://www.adr.it.

Search for: regole bagaglio a mano.
