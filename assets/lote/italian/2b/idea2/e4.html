Attività 4 Un soggiorno in Italia

Now that you have done some preliminary brainstorming for il tuo soggiorno in Italia, it’s time to do some serious planning.

Look at the tips in the travel guide on Vacanze fai da te!

 

Vacanze fai da te!

  1. Decidi quali città vuoi visitare e i posti che vuoi vedere.
  2. Scegli il modo di trasporto.
  3. Prenota la sistemazione che è adatta alle tue esigenze.
  4. Scegli qualche tour.

In this activity you will be planning your trip, then creating a presentation. You may choose to do this attività either on your own or with a partner.

When you are planning, you need to keep the following restrictions in mind.

  • You are on a limited budget, so when you make your choices you will need to take into consideration what you can afford.
  • You have $5,000 AUD to cover your land costs in Italy. This includes accommodation, transport within Italy, tours, entertainment and food. It does not include your flights to and from Italy, course fees (if you are doing a language course) and shopping.
  • You need to spend an extended amount of time in one place, for example:
    • a holiday job, perhaps working in una gelateria on the beach
    • a study course at a language school.
  • The length of il soggiorno is around 4–5 weeks.

Una guida: Un soggiorno in Italia

  1. Città/regione

    • città/regione principale
    • altre città/regioni

    On a map of Italy, mark out the route you will take to the places you are going to visit.

    Highlight the main location of il tuo soggiorno.


    Try to plan a direct route between the places you would like to visit. If you are not going backwards and forwards, it will save you both time and money. This is important especially as you have limited time and budget!

  1. Motivo principale

    • vacanza studio
    • vacanza lavoro
    • solo vacanza
    • altro

    What is your main reason for being in Italy? Find the information you need to help you to get started, ie study course/language school, casual job opportunities, etc.

  1. Sistemazione

    Che tipo di sistemazione cerchi per la maggior parte del tuo soggiorno?

    • ostello della gioventù
    • pensione
    • camera in famiglia/con altri ragazzi
    • altro

    Search for the type of accommodation you require. Make a note of:

    • nome
    • dov’è situato
    • prezzo.

    You may wish to focus on the accommodation where you will be spending the most time.


    Don’t forget to check the cost for the period of time you are there. Prices will vary depending on the season.

  2. Trasporto

    Come ti sposterai in Italia?

    • treno
    • motorino
    • bicicletta
    • autobus
    • a piedi

    Write details of the means of transport you intend to use.

    Which discount cards can you buy for public transport?

    If you intend to hire a car, scooter, etc – what is the cost?

  3. Cosa c’è da vedere e fare?

    Approfitta del tuo soggiorno in Italia e cerca di vedere e fare il più possibile.

    Cosa ti interessa? Quali sono le tue passioni?

    • monumenti, chiese, gallerie d’arte, ecc
    • sciare, andare in discoteca, shopping, ecc
    • un evento particolare – il Motorshow, il Giro d’Italia, il Carnevale, la Biennale, ecc

    Select a tour of interest – it’s always a good idea to choose something memorable!

    Search for sightseeing tours, activities and events of interest to you. Make a note of the details.

    You may wish to focus on the places where you will be staying the longest time.

Having extensive knowledge about what there is to see allows you to take advantage of the unique tourist opportunities available in a particular country.

Be well-informed about any popular events taking place during your stay. It may be the only opportunity you will have in your lifetime to experience a particular occasion!

Presentation

  • Choose the mode of presentation that suits you best.
  • Write your notes/information in italiano.

Buon viaggio!
