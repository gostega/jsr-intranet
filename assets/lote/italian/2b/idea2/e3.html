Attività 3 Sconti e agevolazioni

The next step in your travel plans is to check out which discount cards are available per i giovani in Italia.

To find out this information, a good starting point is the Vacanze Fai Da Te website.

Go to http://www.vacanzefaidate.com select Organizzare. Select Studeni tips + tricks

You can also search for information about discount cards on www.yahoo.it.

You may find the information on the websites difficult to understand. As an Italian government site, it uses formal language.

You may find yourself in situations where you will need to make meaning of authentic, high level language. This does not mean that you have to understand everything! You need to focus on specific information relevant to your purpose.

This information should help you to make choices regarding accommodation and transport.

For further useful travel information, you can go to http://www.smarttraveller.gov.au.


Use the websites to help you answer the questions in this worksheet.


When you have finished, check the answers to see how well you did.
