I motorini in Italia

Nelle sempre più caotiche città italiane il motorino ha rivoluzionato la vita e il modo di spostarsi di moltissimi teenager.

I ragazzi from the age of 14 are allowed to ride scooters. This gives them independence as they don’t have to rely on adults or public transport to get around.

One of the best known brand names of motorini is the Vespa®. Il motorino is easy to manoeuvre through the hectic traffic and you don’t need much space to park! This is a real advantage if you live in an apartment in a large city.

Scooters are a popular means of transport in Italy and cheap to run. With the arrival on the market of the very cost-effective and environmentally friendly electric scooter, it appears that scooters will be the choice of the future.
