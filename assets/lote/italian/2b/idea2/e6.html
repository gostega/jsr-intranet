Attività 6 Prenotare una camera

An important aspect of the travel experience is accommodation. It’s not a good idea to leave booking until the last minute. This may prove costly and you may not get the best deal!


Don’t forget the tips on accommodation in the travel guide in Vacanze fai da te!

Prenota la sistemazione che è adatta alle tue esigenze.


Some hotels will take bookings online.

The kinds of details that you may be asked to provide are:

  • tipologia camera
  • disponibilità
  • data check-in
  • data check-out.

You can view authentic examples at:

www.booking.com (Select Italian as the language.)

www.ostelli.it

www.ostellionline.org

 


At this stage of your planning, you will have identified the accommodation you will be staying in. Now it’s time to make a booking.

Not all hotels and accommodation providers will have the latest online booking facilities. It is therefore important that you know how to make a written request for a hotel booking. Once you are familiar with the language and the format required, you can re-use it for other bookings. All you need to do is to change the dates and other relevant details.

The following example will provide you with a structure that you can use to help you with la tua prenotazione.

To: istitutosantabarbara@katianet

Subject: Prenotazione camera

Istituto Santa Barbara

Via Giulia n5 (Campo dei Fiori)

Roma 00186

Egregio Sig. Direttore

Mi chiamo Alicia Scott e sono una studentessa australiana. Verrò a Roma dove farò un corso di lingua con un’amica nel mese di gennaio. Staremo a Roma quindici notti. Arriveremo la mattina del 5 e partiremo il 20 gennaio.

Vorrei prenotare una camera doppia a due letti. Vorrei sapere la possibilità di questa sistemazione e quali condizioni mi potrebbe offrire per il nostro soggiorno a Roma. Quali sono i prezzi per il pernottamento inclusa colazione o mezza pensione?

In attesa di una Sua cortese risposta.

La ringrazio in anticipo.

Distinti saluti

Alicia Scott

ascott@iinet.net


Mezza pensione – includes breakfast plus one meal (usually dinner).

Pensione completa – includes all meals.

Case religiose

In Rome, religious institutions are being used increasingly as pensioni. With fewer and fewer people joining religious orders, there are often spare rooms available. Letting out rooms provides these institutions with an income while the nuns or monks continue to live there. Consequently, they are safe and clean. They are often found in great locations and offer reasonably priced accommodation. When you are travelling in Italy, it’s a good idea to check out this accommodation option.
