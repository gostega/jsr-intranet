Attività 8 La fiera dei viaggi

Il settore Viaggi e vacanze è sempre in crescita. Di conseguenza, in questo mercato cresce anche la concorrenza.

The travel sector is advertising products increasingly online in order to have greater exposure.

In this attività you will have the opportunity to look at some Internet travel sites.

La fiera dei viaggi is a travel agency that operates online.

Go to www.lafieradeiviaggi.it to access its website.


If you have difficulty accessing this site, try a general search for Viaggi e vacanze. Select one or a few websites to help you to answer the following questions.



Download this worksheet and use it to guide your responses to the websites you have found.



When you have finished the worksheet, check the answers to see how well you did.


Budget travel

Le fiere sono un mezzo per esibire le ultime tendenze e gli ultimi pacchetti e prodotti di viaggio. Ci sono diverse esigenze, diversi budget e gusti.

‘No frills’ travel is an interesting aspect of travel, especially for young people who are usually on a budget.

Budget travel is generally geared towards young people.

As you will likely experience this type of travel at some stage in your life, it’s a good idea to know what to expect. Non vuoi delle brutte sorprese!!! Quali sono le caratteristiche di ‘No frills’ (budget) travel? Informati!

Find out what the main characteristics of budget travel are.

  1. To find out which exhibitions are available in the budget travel category, go to: http://www.abcfiere.com to begin your search. Write as many characteristics as you can. What other aspects do you want to add? Put them in ‘Altro’. See the last column in the worksheet.

  2. Search the Internet for sites that can help you with the information you require. Scrivi in italiano.
