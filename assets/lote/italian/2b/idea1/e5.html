Attività 5 L’intervista

Imagine that you are walking per le vie della città in Italia. You are approached by Marco who asks if you would like to answer some questions about teenage travel.

Take part in the interview. You need to let him know that you are in Italy on holidays and that you come from Australia.

Listen to Marco’s questions. How will you respond to them?

  1. Watch the video segment by clicking on the PLAY button below.
  2. After each question, use the PAUSE button to give yourself time to make your own response to Marco.
  3. You may like to record your voice or even make a video of yourself responding.
  4. Listen to your answers. You are encouraged to do the interview several times to help you to improve your response and also to develop your confidence in speaking.



To help you with your answers, refer back to how Federico and Valentina answered the same questions. It’s a good idea to prepare your answers before you do the interview.
