Attività 3 Viaggi Junior

Viaggiare è importante anche per i bambini. Da grandi, può insegnare loro a diventare buoni viaggiatori e aperti a nuove esperienze. Junior travel is considered to be so important that there is even a travel fiera dedicated soley to i viaggiatori junior.

To find out further information about exhibitions specific to travel for children, visit www.childrenstour.com.

If you have problems accessing this site, go to: http://www.abcfiere.com which is the portal for fiere in Italia.

On this site you can find information about exhibitions in general.

You can search for details about ‘children’s tour’ and all other exhibitions on this site.
