Voglia di vacanza

Attività 7 Come vivono le vacanze i teenager australiani?

Tu e i tuoi amici dove andate in vacanza? Vivete le vacanze nello stesso modo dei vostri coetanei italiani?

To find out about Australian teenage holiday trends, prepare a list of questions to ask students in your Italian class.


If you have a link with another local Italian class, discuss the topic during a chat session. It will give you a more comprehensive representation.


Aim for about ten questions. Clicca qui to view possible questions for the chat session.


If you need help and ideas regarding what questions to ask, listen again to the questions asked in the interview in Attività 2.


L’intervista

  1. Interview at least two students and make a note of their responses.
  2. Record their answers in the worksheet provided.
  3. It is a good idea to make a written, audio or video recording of the interview so that you can go back and access the responses.

Alternative scenario

The students from your sister school in Italy have been asked to find out where i ragazzi in Australia like to go and what they like to do on holiday. They have asked your Italian class to provide information on the topic. Each student has been asked to write to their e-compagno di classe.

Spoken interaction

In order to find out about the Australian teenage holiday trend and favourite destinations, develop a survey for your peers. Record the answers. Write a summary of your findings.
