Il periodo ipotetico

Il periodo ipotetico is a structure composed of two clauses.

  1. The if clause indicates a condition. In Italian, se introduces the condition.
  2. Main clause indicates the result of that condition.

Generally you will not be expected to use il periodo ipotetico. You will come across it, however in texts. It's important that you are able to understand its meaning when used in texts.

Look at the following examples.
