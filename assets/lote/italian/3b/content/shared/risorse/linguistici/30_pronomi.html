I pronomi diretti

Direct object pronouns replace a noun that has already been mentioned in a phrase.

Look at the following examples.

I pronomi (lo, la, li and le)

lo – it (masculine)
replaces a masculine word in the singular		 Mangio il pesce ogni tanto. Lo mangio ogni tanto.
I eat it every now and again.
(lo replaces il pesce)
la – it (feminine)
replaces a feminine word in the singular		 Mangio la carne spesso. La mangio spesso.
I eat it often.
(la replaces la carne)
li – them (masculine)
replaces a masculine word in the plural		 Evito i dolci quando è possible. Li evito quando è possible.
I avoid them when possible.
(li replaces i dolci)
le – them (feminine)
replaces a feminine word in the plural		 Mangio le uova spesso. Le mangio spesso.
I eat them often.
(le replaces Ie uova)

Other pronomi diretti

mi me ci us
ti you (singular) vi you (plural)
lo him/it (masculine) li them (masculine)
la her/it (feminine) le them (feminine)
La you (polite singular) Li you (polite plural – masculine)
    Le you (polite plural – feminine)

Direct object pronouns:

  • replace the object of the verb.
  • generally go before the verb.
  • are attached to the end of an infinitive; the final –e of the infinitive is dropped.

Non hai mangiato tutta la pizza?
Mi dispiace non riesco a finirla. Sono pieno.

In a negative sentence the word non goes before the direct object pronoun. For example:
Mangi la carne ogni giorno?
No, non la mangio molto spesso.


I pronomi diretti con il passato prossimo

With il passato prossimo and other compound tenses, the direct object pronoun remains in front of the verb. The ending of the past participle agrees in gender and number with the noun to which it refers.

  • Compound tenses are made of two parts: an auxiliary verb (essere or avere) and the past participle of the verb in question.
  • Lo and la drop the vowel and are replaced by an apostrophe when in front of the conjugated forms of avere.


Ho visto tuo fratello. L’ho visto. I saw him.
Ho visto tua sorella. L’ho vista. I saw her.
Ho visto i tuoi fratelli. Li ho visti. I saw them.
Ho visto le tue sorelle. Le ho viste. I saw them.
