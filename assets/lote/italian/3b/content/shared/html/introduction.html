Introduction

Welcome to Italian Unit 3B – ...e poi?

In ...e poi? you will reflect on your personal and cultural identity and examine aspects of Italian identity.

You will discuss your aspirations and plans for the future and explore future study, employment and travel including holiday job opportunities in Italy.

Issues that face the world and initiatives to address them in your local environment as well as in Italy and globally will be addressed.

Modern technology will allow you to be transported into the heart of Italian life, thus providing you with the current Italian reality.

This resource contains five topics. These topics are accessible by clicking on the menu on the left-hand side.

How to navigate this resource

Each topic has four separate sections which can be accessed by clicking the tabs at the top of the screen. These sections are:

  • Attività

  • Print content
