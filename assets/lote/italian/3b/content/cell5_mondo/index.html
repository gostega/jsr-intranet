Il mondo nelle nostre mani addresses issues and challenges that face our world and society.

You will engage with a variety of texts that expose different facets of world issues. Some interesting examples are songs and a personal account of a youth’s involvement in a humanitarian cause.

This exposure will help to develop your awareness of issues and initiatives in your local environment as well as at a global level.

You will be challenged to take action and help move towards un mondo migliore.

Suggested time for working through this topic is twelve hours.
