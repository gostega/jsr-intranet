Mathias e Amadou

 

Mathias e Amadou, a publication by the European Commission, aims to raise children's awareness about world cooperation and development. The message it conveys is that, as we all live in the same world, it’s only just that the wealthy nations help the less fortunate ones.

The publication is available free of charge online at http://bookshop.europa.eu, go to [IT] Basta un clic per le pubblicazioni dell’UE, select Italiano(it) in the language box at top, then put ‘Mathias e Amadou’ in the QuickSearch box and click the Go button.

You can get the publication in printed format or download the PDF document.

Read the publication. Even though it’s dealing with a complex topic, it has been written in a simple way. It will provide you with vocabulary appropriate to the theme and help to develop your Italian language skills.

Then write your comments about the publication like a book review.

You may wish to focus on a current, topical issue of your choice.

Select a text that interests you and explore its content.

The type of response will depend on the text you have chosen. Choose from the following:

Summary: Write summary notes on content of the text.

Review: Write a review for an online audience of a book/excerpt that you have read.

Email: Write an email to the author of the book/project and express your thoughts about the text.

Letter: Write a letter to an organisation/online magazine in which you express your views on the issue raised in the text.

Discussion: Discuss with other speaker/s the issue/s highlighted in the text.

Scrapbook: Compile a selection of images relevant to Problemi del mondo. Paste them in a scrapbook and write captions. You can also use your collection to engage in conversation.

Other: Choose your own format that is appropriate for the text that you have selected.
