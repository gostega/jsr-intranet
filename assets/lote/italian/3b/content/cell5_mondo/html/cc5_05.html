Puoi fare e dare di più!

In the topic Il mondo nelle nostre mani you have been exposed to various aspects of world issues.

In this attività you will be challenged to make yourself heard, to look at an issue from a personal perspective and, hopefully, to take action.

TakingITGlobal is an online world youth community. Visit http://www.takingitglobal.org.

The site will give you an idea of how young people get involved in their local community. You may be inspired to become part of this community and strive to make a difference!

Select italiano for language. This will help to extend your reading skills. (You will find the selection at the bottom of the home page.)

Partecipa a una discussione

 

Partecipa a una discussione su un argomento di particolare interesse ai giovani nel tuo giro di amicizie,Take part in a youth panel discussion/debate on a topic of special interest to young people in your friendship group, nell’ambito scolastico o nella tua città/regione.your school environment or your city or region.

L’argomento deve essere relativo al temaThe topic has to relate to the theme, Il mondo nelle nostre mani.

Argomento suggerito

Il community service (servizio per la comunità) a scuola

Cosa si fa nella tua scuola per il community service?What do you do for community service in your school?

In quali attività possono essere coinvolti gli studenti?Ad esempio, visite a case di riposo.What activities can students be involved in? For example, visits to nursing homes.

Cosa pensi del community service?What do you think about community service?

Secondo te, quali sono i benefici del servizio per la comunità per la formazione dei giovani?What are the benefits of community service in the development of young people?

È bene che faccia parte del curriculum scolastico?Is it good that it’s part of the school curriculum?

Pensi che debba essere facoltativo?Do you think that it should be optional?

Qual è la reazione degli studenti a questa parte del curriculum?What is the reaction of students to this part of the curriculum?


For information on community service in schools that will help your discussion, refer to http://bswb.det.wa.edu.au. Select Student engagement, then select Community service, then Planning and implementation, and Resource kit.

Of particular interest is the Presentation for students available in the resource kit.

To find out more about community service in schools, go to www.curriculum.wa.edu.au, select Senior Secondary > Community Service Program.

Il valore del World Youth Day.


You may already have covered some issues in other areas of the curriculum. You may select one of these as it will help you with the content for discussion.

Prepare thoroughly for this attività. It will help you become confident with your content and performance.

Following are four questions to help direct your thoughts.

  • Qual è l’argomento?What is the issue?
  • Qual è la situazione attuale?What is the present situation?
  • Qual è il tuo punto di vista sull’argomento?What is your view on the topic?
  • Che cosa si può fare per affrontare la situazione?What action can be taken to begin to address the situation?

Keep in mind that these are only a guideline. Depending on the topic you choose, some may not be relevant and you may need to create some of your own.

To help you with the discussion, review Partecipare a una discussione and Esprimere un’opinione
