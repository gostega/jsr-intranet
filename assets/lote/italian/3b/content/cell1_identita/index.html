Identità

Identità is about identity. It explores personal and cultural identity in a variety of contexts including cyberspace.

In this topic you will engage with interesting, authentic texts including information by specialists in the field. This exposure will develop your confidence in dealing with more sophisticated language. You will write on topics of increasing complexity for an online audience and take part in discussions related to the theme of Identità.

Suggested time for working through this topic is twelve hours.
