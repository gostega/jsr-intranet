Aspirazioni e sogni

Aspirazioni e sogni addresses the aspirations and hopes of young people. As you are about to embark on a new phase of your life, you have the opportunity to think about and share hopes and plans for the future with other young people.

You will participate in youth panel discussions and forums, research opportunities to study in Italy and complete an application for a scholarship. These engaging activities allow you to use language in meaningful contexts.

Suggested time for working through this topic is twelve hours.
