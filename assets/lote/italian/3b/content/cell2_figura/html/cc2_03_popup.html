About the author

Beppe Severgnini is a writer and columnist for Italy’s largest circulation daily newspaper, Corriere della Sera. He worked as a correspondent in London for a number of years and currently is back in Milan.

This experience of living abroad gives him the advantage of being both an observer of Italians but also an involved, empathetic citizen who has a deep understanding of them.

Severgnini has a strong presence on the Internet. He has his own forum site on the newspaper’s website, www.corriere.it, under the Opinioni link. The Italians is a forum instrumental in establishing links for Italians living abroad and enabling them to express their opinion on topical aspects of life in Italy.
