Why might the Italian public react differently to foreign readers to the content of the books? Give reasons to support your view.

Italian audience Foreigners/outsiders
  • Might feel insulted/threatened by negative comments made about Italians.
  • Might not be able to laugh at themselves.
  • Stereotypical representation is too general and static. Society is fluid and changes with time.
  • Difficult to remove yourself from culture that you are immersed in. Take it for granted. Might not be aware of some of the views portrayed by the author.
  • Can be objective and detached. Their own culture is not being commented on or under scrutiny.
  • Foreigners/outsiders are more likely to feed off stereotypes.
  • Audience does not have a deep understanding of intrinsic cultural aspects of make up of Italians.
  • Can be seen as a type of manual for outsiders wanting to gain an insight into the Italian way of life and thinking.
