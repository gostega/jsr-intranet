Spazio lavoro

Spazio lavoro explores a variety of facets of the world of work.

You will engage in practical activities which include searching for holiday job opportunities in Italy, and writing a curriculum vitae and a job application. These skills are transferable to real-life situations.

Suggested time for working through this topic is eleven hours.
