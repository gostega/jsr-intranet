I verbi suonare e giocare

Suonare – to play (a musical instrument)

*Giocare – to play (a sport)

In English there is no distinction. To play is used for both sport and musical instruments.

*Giocare is slightly different from regular –are verbs.

You need to add an h to the tu and noi forms.

tu giochi
noi giochiamo
