How would you answer questions on the topic of Il fitness and Una dieta sana in an assessment situation?

Here are some tips.

Preparation

  • Revise regularly material covered in the attività.
  • Try to incorporate relevant words and phrases into your responses.

During the assessment

  • Ensure your responses are appropriate to the questions asked. Be flexible. The questions asked may not be exactly what you are used to. Adapt the language you know to answer the question asked.
  • Extend and give detail in your answers. Giving a ‘sì‘ or ’no‘ answer may show that you understand the question. It does not demonstrate how much language you know!
