Attività 3 Sondaggio – Sei in forma?

Cosa fanno i tuoi coetani per mantenersi in forma?

Develop a survey to find out what your peers do to keep fit.

Be creative!
  • Create some original questions of your own.
  • Have a title –a possibility is Sei in forma?
    You are free and encouraged to choose your own title!
  • Choose the form of media to present il tuo sondaggio, for example online, print.

Some sample questions have been provided to help you get started. Refer to Una guida: Sei in forma? You may use some of these as they are but you can also modify them.

Refer to Mantenersi in forma for a list of relevant espressioni e parole.

Refer to Le domande if you need to revise how to form questions.
