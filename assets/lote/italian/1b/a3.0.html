Musica e cinema!

This section focuses on the entertainment theme specific to la musica e il cinema and allows you to indulge in this wonderful world! Che bello!!

Listening to music, going to concerts and the cinema are very popular teenage activities.

You can find out about other teenagers’ personal taste in music and films. You will be invited to go on a discovery tour of the Italian music and film scene and be transported into this aspect of the world of Italian teenagers. You will have the opportunity to access Italian radio and fi lm through the Internet.

The effect of digital technology in the music and film world will also be examined.


You will:
  • talk about the music preferences of young people
  • look at the digital generation
  • organise to go to a concert
  • talk about films and the cinema
  • learn about La Notte Bianca a Roma
  • learn about dates
  • revise how to ask questions.

Work through the activities. Go to Extra for activities that will allow you to access authentic Italian materials on the topic.

The suggested time for working through this section is seven sessions.
