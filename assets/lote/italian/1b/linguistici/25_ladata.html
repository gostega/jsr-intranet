La data

To say the date in Italian you need to remember the following points:

  • the days and months do not have a capital letter
  • with the exception of il primo (the first), all other dates are expressed as cardinal numbers. In English, ordinal numbers are used to express dates, for example:

    • il due novembre
    il quattro gennaio
    il dieci dicembre

    Exception

    il primo marzo
    (primo is an ordinal number)

  • for most dates you add the masculine article il in front of the number, for example:
    • il sette ottobre
  • for numbers starting with a vowel, such as otto and undici, the article changes to l’, for example:
    • l’undici ottobre
    l’otto maggio.
