Vivere bene

This second section explores the importance of a healthy lifestyle for il benessere of teenagers.

This section is divided into two parts:

  • Il fitness explores ways of keeping fit and the roles sport and physical activity play in the lives of teenagers. The focus is on the importance of physical activity for il benessere and the need for this awareness is addressed.

  • Una dieta sana looks at what constitutes a healthy diet and the importance of mangiare bene. The eating habits of teenagers both in Australia and Italy will be looked at. You will be able to compare this aspect of youth culture to your own reality.
