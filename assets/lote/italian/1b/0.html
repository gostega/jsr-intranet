Benvenuti all’italiano!

Italian Unit 1B consists of two sections Mondo da vivere and Vivere bene.

Mondo da vivere

This first section explores various aspects of youth culture both in Italy and in Australia in a variety of contexts. It provides an insight into how Italian teenagers prefer to spend their leisure time, which in turn will enable students to compare it to their own reality. Aspects of modern means of teenage communication are embedded throughout the course.

Un po' di relax looks at the teenage world of tempo libero and associated leisure activities. The importance of friends is also addressed.
Un mare di divertimenti explores the going out scene which is an exciting component of the teenage reality. It also looks at holiday activities and cultural exchange opportunities which you may want to pursue.
Musica e cinema looks at teenagers’ preferences regarding music and films. You will be invited to access Italian radio and cinema.

Vivere bene

The second section explores the importance of a healthy lifestyle for teenagers.
Il fitness examines ways to keep fit and the importance of exercise.
Una dieta sana deals with healthy eating and the importance of a balanced diet.
