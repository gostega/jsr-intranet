Attività 5 Esercizi linguistici

I verbi riflessivi

Esercizio 1

Complete the following sentences with the correct reflexive pronoun.

Identify the verb first. Then select the correct reflexive pronoun that is required for the form of the verb. For example:

[ Ti ] diverti a scuola?

The verb is diverti. This is the tu form. The reflexive pronoun required for this form is ‘Ti’.

As a hint, the verbs in the sentences are the first word following the missing reflexive pronouns.
