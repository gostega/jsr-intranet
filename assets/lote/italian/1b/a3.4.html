    Esercizio 8 – A che ora?

    A che ora incominciano i concerti?

    Write out fully in Italian the time of the following concerts. Use the 24-hour clock!

    To say that something is on at a particular time, we use the word alle in front of the number, for example:
    • Il concerto è alle 19:00. (The concert is at 7:00pm.)
    • La festa incomincia alle venti. (The party begins at 8:00pm.)
    Exceptions
    • a mezzogiorno (at midday)
    • a mezzanotte (at midnight)
    • all’una (at one o’clock)
