Attività 5 Hai una dieta sana?

I tuoi amici hanno una dieta sana?

To find out you will need to ask them some questions. Prepare a survey on the topic of una dieta sana.

The following are some sample questions.

You can modify the questions and include your own as well. Be creative!

Una guida: Hai una dieta sana?

Hai una dieta sana? Do you have a healthy diet?
Mangi frutta e verdura ogni giorno/tutti i giorni? Do you eat fruit and vegetables every day?
Quante porzioni? How many portions/servings?
Quanta acqua bevi al giorno? How much water do you drink a day?
Mangi molti dolci?
Quali?
Quanto spesso?		 Do you eat many sweets?
Which ones?
How often?
Do you have snacks?
What do you eat?		 Fai spuntini?
Cosa mangi?
Ti piace il fast food?
Quale preferisci?
Quanto spesso lo mangi?		 Do you like fast food?
Which one do you prefer?
How often do you eat it?

Prepare your questions in this template. Then conduct the survey with at least three other people and record their answers. Make another copy if you can find more people to participate.

After you have completed the survey, write a brief summary of your findings.

The following expressions are useful in helping you write the summary.

Secondo il mio sondaggio ...According to my survey ...
Ho notato che ... I have noticed that ...
È interessante notare/sapere ...It is interesting to note/see ...
Il cibo preferito è ...The favourite food is ...
Molti ragazzi mangiano frutta e verdura tutti i giorni.Many teenagers eat fruit and vegetables every day.
A tutti piacciono i dolci.Everyone likes sweets.
Il pesce piace a pochi.Only a few like fish.
Il fast food piace a due persone.Two people like fast food.

Refer also to La dieta for other useful words and phrases.
