Impersonal expressions

These are usually followed by a verb in the infinitive form.

For example:

  • È preferibile evitare il fast food.
  • È essenziale mangiare frutta e verdure ogni giorno.

You are not expected to understand this concept at this stage. You are just required to recognise and use the expressions only as required in the context of Una dieta sana.

Impersonal Si construction

When translating into English there are several equivalents for one, you (used in generic sense) we, they or people + verb.

The more common forms used in English are 'one' and 'you'.

For example:

  • Si deve bere molta acqua.
  • Si deve evitare il cibo grasso.
