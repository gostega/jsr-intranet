You can also use your dictionary to help you. Remember that (nm) masculine and (nf) feminine after a noun indicates its gender.

La preposizione ‘di’

When talking about food you may need to use the word ‘some’ to express quantities and amounts.
In Italian the word di + articulated form is used to express the word ‘some’.

Refer to Preposizioni articolate to revise how this is done.
