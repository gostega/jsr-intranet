Attività 7 Un incontro con gli studenti di scambio

Imagine that a group of exchange students is visiting your school. You will have the opportunity to meet them and get to know them.

The focus is on youth culture.

What a fantastic way to find out about each other! It is very personal, real and above all molto autentico! Che bello!
To maximise this fantastic opportunity to practise your Italian and find out about Italian youth culture make sure that you are well prepared for this incontro.

Task

Parte 1: Le risposte

What can you say about your passatempi e tempo libero?

What can you say about the leisure activities of typical Australian teenagers?

Prepare answers to possible questions related to personal and teenage leisure time activities. You may want to focus on students at your school or friends.

The following guideline provide possible questions that i ragazzi italiani may ask you.


Una guida: Un incontro con gli studenti di scambio

Cosa ti piace fare quando hai tempo libero? What do you like to do when you have spare time?
Cosa fai dopo la scuola? What do you do after school?
Cosa fai il weekend? What do you do at/on the weekend?
Cosa ti piace fare con gli amici? What do you like to do with friends?
Qual’è il tuo passatempo preferito? What is your preferred/favourite pastime?
Cosa piace fare ai ragazzi nella tua scuola? What do the guys (students) at your school like to do?
Cosa fanno i giovani austaliani nel loro tempo libero? What do young Australians do in their spare time?

How would you answer the questions? Refer to information from the previous activities to help you. Prepare written answers and rehearse them before l’incontro.

If you have recording facilities, record your answers and play them back so that you can listen to them.
