What is the situation like in Italy?

Rete Giovani is running a series of programs on La salute dei giovani in Italia. The first program is I ragazzi italiani sono in forma?

A journalist interviews a teenager in the street in Italy to find out what teenagers do to keep fit.

Vediamo come sono le cose in Italia!

Use the worksheet B1.7 to take notes while you watch the video.

How much of the interview are you able to understand?

If you don’t understand much of the interview, non ti preoccupare! You are not expected to.

Remember that you are listening to a native speaker so the speed may be fast. The reality is that it is normal speed for a native speaker! It is probably the same speed that you speak in English.

To help you understand the content:
  • focus on the questions
  • make notes in point form of words/phrases that you are able to understand
  • look at body language – this may also help you make meaning of the content
  • watch the video several times to familiarise yourself with the voices and the content.
