Esercizio 4

Listen to the deejay again.

  • Sono la deejay Paola.
  • La mia domanda oggi è: Perché è così bello stare insieme agli amici?
  • Ragazzi chiamatemi. Ecco alcuni motivi.
  • Ascoltiamo le opinioni di alcuni ragazzi.

Why don't you join in? How would you answer the questions?

Express your opinion in Italian. Practise writing your answers first.

Record your response if you have recording facilities (mp3 player ecc).

When speaking about likes and preferences the verb preferire is commonly used.

The verb preferire has a different pattern from the regular –ire verbs. Refer to I verbi –ire (–isc) to find out how it is conjugated.
