Per finire

By now you should be aware of some of the differences that exist between the Italian and Australian youth cultures.

While finding out about the Italian reality, you have also been challenged to think about your own reality!

Imagine that you have been successful in your application for a language scholarship.

You have been awarded la borsa di studio and you will be going to Italy on exchange at the end of the year. There are so many things that you need to do to prepare yourself for this experience of a lifetime!

Begin by preparing for the school experience. Gli studenti italiani will want to know all about the Australian teenage culture.

Vuoi fare bella fi gura!

Task

Prepare a presentation on the Australian teenage leisure culture which you can show students when you are at school in Italy.

Think of your target audience and use an appropriate delivery mode, for example Powerpoint® web page.

Include visual material where possible. You may also wish to include some street interview scenes.

The content should focus on the following areas:

  • pastimes
  • weekend outings/activities
  • holiday activities/destinations.

This attività requires you to use the language that you have covered in this section.

Tips for writing in Italian

When you read:
  • note down any interesting phrases and new vocabulary that you could use in your own writing
  • write in italiano as much as possible – correspond with an Italian e-pal
  • make good use of the material presented in the activities – this material provides you with a model and the necessary structures
  • check language points that you are not sure of in Elementi linguistici
  • check spellings of words and phrases in Come si dice and in your dizionario.

Una sfida

Include any other information that you consider to be relevant and that you think you are able to express in Italian.

Extend your answers as much as possible but also focus on linguistic accuracy.
