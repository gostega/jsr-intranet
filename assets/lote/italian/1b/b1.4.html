Attività 4 Il fitness personale

Peter, Rachel, Alana e Christian sono giovani australiani.

They have been invited to go on air by the Italian community radio. The radio has a program dedicated to i giovani. It provides a fantastic opportunity for young Italo-Australians and students learning Italian to keep in touch with the language! Che bello!

The deejay interviews the teenagers on Il fitness personale.
Listen to which attività fisiche these teenagers engage in and complete the following exercises. If you are having difficulty understanding, try listening to one teenager at a time. Complete the questions that relate to the person, then move on to the next teenager.
