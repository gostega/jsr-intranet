Attività 6 Gli studenti di scambio parlano del loro tempo libero

A local Italian radio station has invited a group of Italian exchange students to participate in an on air interview. The topic is tempo libero.
Ascoltiamo questi ragazzi che parlano dei loro passatempi preferiti.

When talking about tempo libero, the students used the following parole/espressioni to express when or how often they do the activities.
Quando? When?
di solito usually
sabato sera Saturday night
la domenica Sunday (on Sundays)
a volte sometimes
raramente rarely
quando è when it is possible
il fine settimana weekend

Amare, adorare and odiare are verbs that can be used to express likes and dislikes. They are regular –are verbs. Refer to I verbi –are nel presente if you need to check how these verbs are conjugated.
The verb piacere is used widely to speak about preferences in tempo libero activities. Refer to Il verbo piacere for information on how it works.
