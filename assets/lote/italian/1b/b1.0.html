Il fitness

Il fitness looks at the roles sport and physical activity play in the lives of teenagers. These are generally very popular teenage leisure activities.

You will meet teenagers who speak about their fitness routine and share their opinions on physical activity.

The focus is on the importance of physical activity for il benessere and the need for this awareness is addressed.

La passione per il calcio is examined and you are invited to meet some of i giocatori italiani e australiani. Gol! Gol!

You will:

  • look at typical physical activities in which teenagers engage and their fitness routines
  • learn about the importance of physical activity
  • explore the world of il calcio
  • learn about reflexive verbs
  • revise –isc verbs
  • be introduced to the adverbs molto, troppo and poco.

The suggested time for working through this section is ten sessions.
