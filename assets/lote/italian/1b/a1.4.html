To express opinions and feelings/attitudes about i passatempi, here is a list of relevant aggettivi.
E fantastico. It is fantastic.
E megafantastico. It is really fantastic.
E geniale. it is ingenious (cool).
E forte. It is cool.
E rilassante. It is relaxing.
E eccitante. It is exciting.
E divertente. It is enjoyable.
E stimolante. It is exhilarating.
E faticoso. It is tiring/difficult.
E noioso. It is boring.
The verbs fare and andare are used frequently when speaking about tempo libero. They are irregular verbs. Refer to I verbi irregolari to find out how these verbs are formed.
