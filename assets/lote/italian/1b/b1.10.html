Il calcio – Che passione!

In Italia

Lo sport preferito in Italia senz’altro è il calcio. Molti italiani seguono il calcio. Vanno allo stadio, guardano la partita alla televisione o l’ascoltano alla radio.

Tutte le città italiane hanno almeno una squadra di calcio nella Seria A.

La Nazionale è la squadra che rappresenta l’Italia nella Coppa Mondiale e altre partite a livello internazionale.

La passione per il calcio fa parte della cultura italiana. Questa passione è molto ovvia durante I Mondiali. La vittoria italiana nel 2006 è stata incredibile – la finale è stata piena di suspense, eccitamento e tensione sia per i giocatori sia per i tifosi!

In Australia

Il calcio is becoming an increasingly popular sport in Australia as well. Events like the Soccer World Cup and its wide exposure assist greatly in promoting the sport. It is a month-long feast for soccer lovers but also for the general public who get engrossed in the excitement of this mega event.

In the 2006 World Cup, Australian and Italian giocatori had the chance to meet on the soccer field. In this eventful match, full of gripping and tense moments, Italy managed to score the only goal in the closing minutes of the game!

The performance of the Socceroos in the 2006 World Cup served to lift the profile of Australian soccer and to inspire many young players!

What made this encounter very interesting and difficult for some of the players was that their country's loyalty was put the test.

Marco Bresciano was one of those players. He is Italo-Australian and at the time was playing for an Italian league team.

He chose to play for Australia at I Mondiali del 2006.

Marco Bresciano played a significant role during the 2006 Soccer World Cup. Where is he now? To find out try a general search at www.yahoo.it or use the search engine of your choice.

Marco Bresciano is featured in the ABC television program Another sun – Italy. È molto interessante! If you get the chance, have a look at it.

If you cannot access the TV program, go to www.abc.net.au and search for ‘Another sun – Italy’. You can read the transcript there.
