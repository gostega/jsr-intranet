Tempo libero in Italia!

Andare in centro signifies many things per i giovani italiani. It is the place to meet up with gli amici in piazza, to go and eat a pizza al taglio, prendere un gelato, guardare le vetrine e così via and so on. Very often the main purpose is purely to socialise!! Andare in discoteca is another important aspect of teenage social life.

La febbre di sabato sera! is an Italian teenage phenomenon.

Discos in Italia usually open after 11pm. Unlike in Australia, you don’t have to be 18 to go to a disco.

In Italy there is a difference between night clubs and discos. To get into a night club, there is an age restriction – you have to be 18, the same as in Australia.

Teenagers usually meet before in a pizzeria or the local piazza before going to the disco to socialise and chat with friends.

Being such a late start it is common for teenagers to fare le ore piccole!

There are also under age teenage discos where teenagers as young as 13 can go – these usually start in the late afternoon.
