Ci vediamo in piazza              View the gallery

Most towns in Italy have a piazza. The piazza is generally surrounded by bars, churches, shops and offices. Large cities have numerous piazze, some large and some small.

In Italy getting together and socialising with friends is an important aspect of the way of life.

Often the local piazza is a central place to meet and spend some time together especially in the smaller cities. The piazza is also a place where festivals and events take place.

In Australia the equivalent gathering point for young people perhaps would be the local shopping mall/centre.

Imagine having the famous Piazza di Spagna a Roma with the Spanish steps as your backdrop when meeting friends! It's difficult to imagine but it's a reality for local Roman teenagers!!!

It is just one of the many famous squares in Italy!

Some other well-known piazze include:

  • Piazza Navona a Roma
  • Piazza San Marco a Venezia
  • Piazza del Duomo a Milano
  • Piazza del Campo a Siena
  • Piazza a Bologna
