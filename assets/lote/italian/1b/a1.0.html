Un po’ di relax!

Un po’ di relax explores the teenage world of tempo libero and leisure activities. You will also be introduced to the Italian teenage leisure scene and be able to compare it to your own reality.
Another aspect that is examined is l’importanza degli amici for teenagers.

You will have the opportunity to do a web quest on leisure activities and develop your understanding of Internet conventions with a Netiquette quiz … e così via . E tutto in Italiano – che bello!

The objective is to discover this aspect of the teenage world in a realistic way and to provide meaningful contexts for you to develop your understanding of and competency in Italian.


You will:
  • learn to speak about leisure activities
  • look at what young Italians like to do
  • examine the importance of friends
  • learn the present tense of regular verbs
  • learn the present tense of –ire (–isc) verbs
  • learn common irregular verb

The suggested time for working through this section is 13 sessions.
