Attività 7 Andiamo in discoteca!

Andare in discoteca, come abbiamo già visto è una parte molto importante della vita dei giovani italiani.

Listen to these giovani organising to go to the disco. Listen as often as you need to.
Refer to Invitare to help you to understand the conversation.

Tocca a te!

It's your turn to organise an outing with a friend.

You will need to practise first with another person. Use the role play description cards as a stimulus for your conversation. You have four pairs to choose from, alternate your role with your partner and practice as much as you can.

It is a good idea to write your answers first before doing this activity orally.

After you have had practice using language related to organising an outing, you should be ready for the following task.

Task
