Attività 11 Come hai passato le vacanze?

The holidays you looked forward to so much, are now over!

How did you spend the holidays? Print out the survey to complete for yourself. Then interview your friends using the same questions in the survey to find out how they spent their holidays. Print out a copy of the survey for each friend you are going to interview.

The questions are in the past tense. This is because the holidays are now over and are a past event.

To talk about past events, you have to use il passato prossimo.

You already have seen il passato prossimo and its formation with the verb avere in Attività 5 of this section.

Some verbs take essere to form il passato prossimo. Refer to Il passato prossimo (essere) to find out how this is done.
