Una dieta sana

Una dieta sana is essential for il benessere and should be followed in conjunction with il fitness. In this section you will look at what constitutes a healthy diet and learn the importance of mangiare bene.

La dieta Mediterranea will be looked at as an example of healthy eating while il fast food is seen as being representative of an unhealthy diet.

Teenagers both in Australia and Italy talk about their eating habits and you will learn about the new teenage eating trends in Italy.

You will see a local prize-winning pizzaiolo demonstrate how to make una pizza margherita squisita!

You will:

  • food groups
  • teenage eating habits
  • the Mediterranean diet
  • fast food
  • la pizza al taglio
  • the irregular verb bere
  • the direct object pronouns lo, la, li and le
  • impersonal expressions.

Work through the activities. Go to Extra for activities which will broaden your understanding of Italian.

The suggested time for working through this section is ten sessions.
