Un mare di divertimenti

Un mare di divertimenti focuses on making plans to go out and organising activities.

It also looks at the world of vacanze and explores popular teenage holiday activities including:

  • andare alla spiaggia
  • vacanza-studio and the world of language exchanges.

You will be able to go on an Italian discoteca, apply for a language scholarship and view a typical Italian beach scene in Rimini.

You will learn about the past tense and will be able to speak about past events. From a language perspective this will give you more flexibility and will allow you to use the language with increasing sophistication.


You will:
  • organise to go out
  • talk about holiday activities
  • learn irregular verbs venire and andare
  • learn modal verbs volere, potere and dovere
  • learn the present perfect tense – talking about what you have done
  • learn to talk about time and weather.

Work through the activities. Go to Extra for further activities that will extend and challenge your language acquisition.

The suggested time for working through this section is 15 sessions.
